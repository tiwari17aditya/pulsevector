title :- Daily Current Affairs 31 December 2025:-

1. 1. India officially surpassed Japan to become world’s fourth-largest economy with $4.18 trillion GDP.
2. 2. Finance Ministry formally notified rules allowing 100% FDI in India's insurance sector.
3. 3. Former Bangladesh Prime Minister and BNP Chairperson Khaleda Zia passed away in Dhaka.
4. 4. DRDO successfully conducted the maiden flight test of Pinaka Long Range Guided Rocket-120.
5. 5. US President-elect Donald Trump was awarded Israel Peace Prize following the Mar-a-Lago talks.
6. 6. Anu Garg was appointed as Odisha’s first woman Chief Secretary, succeeding Manoj Ahuja.
7. 7. Bureau of Indian Standards notified IS 19445:2025, India’s first standard for bomb disposal systems.
8. 8. Deepti Sharma became the highest wicket-taker in Women’s T20 International cricket history.
9. 9. Supreme Court ruled that the "Right to Passport" is a part of personal liberty.
10. 10. Bihar’s first Param Rudra supercomputer was inaugurated at IIT Patna under National Supercomputing Mission.
11. 11. India recorded 5.5 lakh trademark registrations in 2024-25, the highest-ever milestone for innovation.
12. 12. Government approved ₹7,000 crore REPM scheme to build a domestic rare-earth magnet ecosystem.
13. 13. China set a world record by propelling a maglev train to 700 kmph.
14. 14. Smriti Mandhana became the second Indian woman to score 10,000 international cricket runs.
15. 15. 8th Pay Commission implementation for Central Government employees was confirmed for January 1, 2026.
