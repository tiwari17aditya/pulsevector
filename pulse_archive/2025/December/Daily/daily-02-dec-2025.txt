Daily Current Affairs [2 Dec 2025]:-

1. 1. Government mandated pre-installation of the Sanchar Saathi security app on all new smartphones to curb mobile fraud.
2. 2. BSF confirmed Pakistan rebuilt terror launchpads along the Jammu frontier, increasing surveillance under Operation Sindoor.
3. 3. Centre will introduce new levies on tobacco and pan masala as the GST compensation cess is set to lapse.
4. 4. Cabinet approved a ₹25,000-crore six-year export mission to support sectors impacted by US tariff measures.
5. 5. Parliament's Winter Session began with discussions, including on the increase in CAT 2025 application fees.
6. 6. Donald Trump's special envoy, Steve Witkoff, held exploratory talks with Vladimir Putin regarding possible Ukraine peace negotiations.
7. 7. Russia criticized NATO's comments about a pre-emptive strike, calling them "irresponsible and escalatory."
8. 8. Jay Shah received the CNN-News18 Indian of the Year 2025 award for Outstanding Achievement in cricket.
9. 9. PM Modi unveiled a 77-foot-tall bronze statue of Lord Rama in Canacona, Goa.
10. 10. India's Real GDP is estimated at ₹48.63 lakh crore in Q2FY26, marking a six-quarter high growth of 8.2%.
11. 11. ISRO Chairman V. Narayanan inaugurated ACEN, India's first private navigation hub in Thiruvananthapuram.
12. 12. National Pollution Control Day and the International Day for the Abolition of Slavery are observed on December 2nd.
13. 13. Hardik Pandya returned from injury to play for Baroda in the Syed Mushtaq Ali Trophy domestic cricket tournament.
14. 14. India's factory activity dropped to a nine-month low due to US tariff measures and weakened demand.
15. 15. Coal India's production fell 3.7% to 453.5 MT in the April-November FY26 period.
