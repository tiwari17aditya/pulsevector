Daily Current Affairs 6 December 2025:-

1. 1. RBI cut the Repo Rate by 25 basis points to 5.25 %, signaling an early policy easing move.
2. 2. Indian Railways commissioned Kavach 4.0, an automatic train protection system, on the Delhi–Mumbai and Delhi–Howrah corridors.
3. 3. IndiGo offered a full refund and free rescheduling on all cancellations from December 5 to 15 due to widespread flight disruptions.
4. 4. India and Russia signed an economic cooperation agreement extending till 2030 in Moscow to boost bilateral trade.
5. 5. Russia assured India of uninterrupted fuel supplies (oil, gas, coal) despite US pressure over oil purchases.
6. 6. The DGCA granted IndiGo a one-time exemption from certain Flight Duty Time Limitation (FDTL) rules until February 10.
7. 7. NITI Aayog and IBM unveiled a roadmap aiming to make India a Top-3 Quantum Economy by 2047.
8. 8. NITI Aayog's Atal Innovation Mission signed an SoI with Hitachi MGRM Net for the 'MStarTM Global AI Connect' platform.
9. 9. The 10th edition of Exercise Garuda Shakti, a joint military exercise between India and Indonesia, began in Bakloh (HP).
10. 10. The World Bank Group and partners helped 375 million people access affordable, quality health services since April 2024.
11. 11. NPCI International Payments Limited (NIPL) signed an MoU with Cambodia's ACLEDA Bank for UPI and KHQR cross-border payments.
12. 12. Sunil Narine became the first cricketer in history to claim 600 wickets in competitive T20 cricket.
13. 13. Vrutti, in partnership with HSBC India, launched 'FPO Shakti' to help Farmer Producer Organisations access capital.
