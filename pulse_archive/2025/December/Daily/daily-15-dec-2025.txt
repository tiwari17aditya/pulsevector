Daily Current Affairs 15 dec 2025:-

1. 1. INSV Kaundinya began a historic voyage to Muscat, recreating the ancient Indo-Arab Sea Trade Route.
2. 2. The Ministry of Jal Shakti launched the Sujalam Bharat App to improve rural water governance under the Jal Jeevan Mission.
3. 3. Kopra Reservoir near Bilaspur became the first ever Ramsar Site from Chhattisgarh state.
4. 4. The Union Cabinet approved the Viksit Bharat Shiksha Adhikshan Bill, proposing a unified higher education regulator.
5. 5. The government is set to introduce a bill to replace MGNREGA with the VB-G Ram G job guarantee scheme, increasing job days to 125.
6. 6. The Geminid Meteor Shower peaked over India between December 13 and 15, offering a spectacular celestial display.
7. 7. Prime Minister Narendra Modi paid tribute to Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel on his 75th death anniversary (December 15, 1950).
8. 8. AIIMS New Delhi conducted India's first clinical trial of the advanced stroke treatment device named the Supernova Stent.
9. 9. The UN Anti-Corruption Conference (COSP11) is scheduled from December 15-19, 2025, in Doha, Qatar.
10. 10. The India International Science Festival (IISF) 2025 was held in New Delhi with the theme: Vigyan Se Samruddhi: for Aatmanirbhar Bharat.
11. 11. Deepavali was inscribed on the UNESCO Representative List of Intangible Cultural Heritage of Humanity in 2025.
12. 12. Nitin Nabin has been appointed as the new National Working President of the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP).
13. 13. India opener Shafali Verma won the ICC Women's Player of the Month award for November 2025.
14. 14. The UAE launched its first officially licensed digital sports wagering platform named Play971.
15. 15. Senior journalist Sangeeta Barooah Pisharoty was elected the first woman president of the Press Club of India.
