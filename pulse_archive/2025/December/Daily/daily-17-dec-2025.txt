title :- Daily Current Affairs 17 Dec 2025:-

1. 1. PM Modi was conferred Ethiopia's highest civilian honour, "The Great Order of the Star of Ethiopia."
2. 2. India ranked 3rd globally in Stanford’s Global AI Vibrancy Tool 2025, trailing only USA and China.
3. 3. India unveiled "DHRUV64," its first indigenous 1.0 GHz dual-core 64-bit microprocessor for self-reliant computing.
4. 4. The Indian Rupee hit a historic record low of 90.87 against the US Dollar in early trade.
5. 5. The Lok Sabha passed the Insurance Laws (Amendment) Bill 2025, raising the FDI limit to 100%.
6. 6. India hosted the Second WHO Global Summit on Traditional Medicine in New Delhi from December 17–19.
7. 7. Neeraj Ghaywan's film "Homebound" was shortlisted for the Oscars 2026 in the Best International Feature category.
8. 8. Russia's President Putin signed a federal law formalizing the Reciprocal Exchange of Logistics Support pact with India.
9. 9. ADB approved a $846 million loan to modernize 650 ITIs across 12 Indian states for skill development.
10. 10. The Indian Army signed an MoU with Raghu Vamsi Group to fast-track indigenous GPS-denied drone capabilities.
11. 11. University of Patanjali became India’s first Yoga and Ayurveda focused ‘Cluster Center’ under Gyan Bharatam Mission.
12. 12. Haryana government announced the formation of the state's 23rd district to improve local administrative efficiency.
13. 13. Iran seized a foreign oil tanker carrying 6 million litres of smuggled diesel in the Gulf of Oman.
14. 14. Indian cricketer Shikhar Dhawan launched his autobiography titled "The One: Cricket, My Life and More" in Delhi.
15. 15. Delhi government banned non-BS VI private vehicles registered outside the capital to curb rising winter air pollution.
