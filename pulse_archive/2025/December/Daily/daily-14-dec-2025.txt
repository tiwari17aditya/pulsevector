title :- Daily Current Affairs 14 December 2025:-

1. 1. National Energy Conservation Day 2025 was observed across India on December 14 to promote energy efficiency awareness.
2. 2. President Droupadi Murmu was the chief guest and felicitated winners of the National Energy Conservation Awards 2025.
3. 3. Bureau of Energy Efficiency (BEE) introduced a new award category for Social Media Influencers this year.
4. 4. Deepavali has been added to UNESCO's List of the Intangible Cultural Heritage of Humanity.
5. 5. The announcement was made during the 20th UNESCO Intergovernmental Committee session hosted in New Delhi.
6. 6. The inclusion of Deepavali signifies it as a living heritage strengthening social bonds and values.
7. 7. India's Men's Junior Hockey Team won the bronze medal at the FIH Men's Junior Hockey World Cup 2025.
8. 8. The team defeated Argentina 4-2 in a remarkable comeback to secure India's first-ever bronze medal in the tournament.
9. 9. The Atomic Energy Bill 2025, or 'SHANTI' Bill, was cleared to allow private and foreign participation in civil nuclear power.
10. 10. Siliserh Lake (Rajasthan) and Kopra Jalashay (Chhattisgarh) were designated as new Ramsar Sites.
11. 11. India launched a National Cyber Safety Awareness Campaign to educate students about online safety and data protection.
12. 12. Indian Railways announced the expansion of Vande Bharat Express services in eastern and northeastern states.
13. 13. The World Health Organization (WHO) launched a global initiative to improve access to mental health services.
14. 14. Andhra Pradesh secured top positions in fruit and fish production, reflecting steady economic growth.
15. 15. Lionel Messi completed the Mumbai leg of his 'GOAT India Tour 2025', meeting Sachin Tendulkar.
