Daily Current Affairs [23 December 2025]:-

1. 1. National Farmers’ Day: India celebrates Kisan Diwas to honor the birth anniversary of former PM Chaudhary Charan Singh.
2. 2. India-NZ FTA: India and New Zealand successfully conclude a historic Free Trade Agreement to boost bilateral trade significantly.
3. 3. Visa Services Suspended: Bangladesh High Commission in New Delhi temporarily suspends all consular services citing security concerns.
4. 4. Assam High Alert: CM Himanta Biswa Sarma places Assam on alert following fresh political unrest in neighboring Bangladesh.
5. 5. Nuclear Bill Assent: President Droupadi Murmu grants assent to SHANTI Bill 2025, opening civil nuclear sectors to private players.
6. 6. Goa Election Results: BJP-led alliance secures decisive victory in Goa Zilla Parishad elections, winning 31 out of 50 seats.
7. 7. National Mathematics Day: Educational institutions across India commemorate the birth anniversary of legendary mathematician Srinivasa Ramanujan.
8. 8. Viksit Bharat Bill: President approves VB-G RAM G Bill 2025, a significant milestone transforming rural employment policies.
9. 9. Kashmir Winter: "Chillai Kalan," the harshest 40-day winter period, begins in the Kashmir Valley with sub-zero temperatures.
10. 10. Aviation Probe: Authorities order investigation into Air India Boeing 777 after it returned to Delhi due to low engine oil.
11. 11. Mining Restriction: Supreme Court rules forest lands cannot be used for non-forestry purposes, including agriculture or mining.
12. 12. SAFF Champions: East Bengal FC (India) wins the inaugural SAFF Women's Club Championship held in Kathmandu, Nepal.
13. 13. U.S.-Greenland Envoy: President Trump reasserts Greenland is vital for national security and appoints a special envoy for the island.
14. 14. Delhi Air Quality: National capital’s AQI slips to 'Very Poor' category as toxic haze blankets the city on Tuesday.
15. 15. Golf Milestone: Noida golfer Sukhman Singh clinches the 124th Amateur Golf Championship of India in a commanding final.
