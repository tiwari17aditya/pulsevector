Daily Current Affairs 3 December 2025:-

1. 1. International Day of Persons with Disabilities is observed annually on December 3rd.
2. 2. RBI appointed Usha Janakiraman as the new Executive Director from December 1, 2025.
3. 3. Punjab National Bank (PNB) named Harmanpreet Kaur as its first-ever female Brand Ambassador.
4. 4. The 14th edition of the India-Maldives Joint Military Exercise EKUVERIN 2025 started in Kerala.
5. 5. India achieved the 7th rank in the World Skills Competition 2025 held in Taiwan.
6. 6. The government launched a Credit Assessment Model (CAM) to strengthen digital loan evaluation for MSMEs.
7. 7. Tamil Nadu received five new GI tags for distinctive products, bringing the state's total to 74.
8. 8. RBI retained SBI, HDFC Bank, and ICICI Bank as Domestic Systemically Important Banks (D-SIBs).
9. 9. The Manipur GST Bill 2025 was passed by the Parliament during the winter session.
10. 10. NHAI collaborated with Reliance Jio for a pilot project on a smart road system.
11. 11. The Delhi High Court dismissed the petitions of wrestlers challenging the WFI elections.
12. 12. Russia praised New Delhi's friendly stand and PM Modi's Ukraine approach ahead of Putin's India visit.
13. 13. FICCI President stated that India is in a 'sweet spot' with GDP growth expected to top 7%.
14. 14. The Department of Land Resources (DoLR) will host a symposium on Naksha and LandStack.
15. 15. Goa observed a public holiday on December 3rd for the Feast of St. Francis Xavier.
