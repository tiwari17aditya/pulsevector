Daily Current Affairs 30 December 2025:-

1. 1. Anti-Terror Conference 2025: Amit Shah inaugurated the event in New Delhi, launching digital databases for crime and weapons.
2. 2. Defence Boost: DAC approved ₹79,000 crore for procurement, including loiter munitions, Pinaka rockets, and Astra Mk-II missiles.
3. 3. Supreme Court Aravalli Stay: SC stayed its previous Aravalli definition ruling and formed an expert panel to assess mining.
4. 4. Australia-India Trade: Piyush Goyal announced zero duty on 100% of Australian tariff lines for Indian exports from January.
5. 5. Eco-Sensitive Zone: Government declared Parvati-Arga Bird Sanctuary in Uttar Pradesh as an Eco-Sensitive Zone to boost biodiversity.
6. 6. India-Russia Space Ties: Russia launched three Iranian satellites (Paya, Kowsar, Zafar-2) from Vostochny Cosmodrome using Soyuz rockets.
7. 7. Electric Tractor Standards: BIS unveiled "19262: 2025," India’s first uniform test code for electric agricultural tractors for safety.
8. 8. Maritime Heritage: PM Modi lauded INSV Kaundinya’s maiden voyage from Porbandar to Muscat, reviving ancient stitched-ship techniques.
9. 9. Cultural Project: Amit Shah inaugurated the ₹227 crore Batadrava cultural project in Assam at Sankardeva’s birthplace.
10. 10. Hurun Rich List: Jayshree Ullal topped the Hurun India Rich List 2025 among first-generation women wealth creators.
11. 11. Malaria Eradication: Amit Shah stated India is near eradicating malaria following a 97% drop in cases nationwide.
12. 12. Assam Internet Restored: Mobile internet services were restored in Karbi Anglong districts after five days of unrest.
13. 13. Global Diplomacy: Trump and Zelensky reported 95% progress on a peace deal after high-stakes talks in Florida.
14. 14. Chess Achievement: Arjun Erigaisi and Koneru Humpy won bronze medals at the FIDE World Rapid Chess Championship.
15. 15. Hemp Policy: India focused on industrial hemp and rare-earth element reserves (6.9M tonnes) for strategic autonomy.
