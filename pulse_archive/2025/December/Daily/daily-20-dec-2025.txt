Daily Current Affairs 20 December 2025

1. 1. International Human Solidarity Day 2025: Observed globally on 20 December to emphasize unity and collective action for social justice.
2. 2. PSU Transformation Awards 2025: Maharatna REC Limited secured four top awards for Cybersecurity, ESG Leadership, Risk Management, and Digital Innovation.
3. 3. Ex-Agniveer Reservation Hiked: Union Home Ministry increased the reservation for ex-Agniveers in CAPF Group C posts from 10% to 50%.
4. 4. India's Largest Circular Labyrinth: Archaeologists discovered a 2,000-year-old classical circular labyrinth with 15 circuits in Solapur, Maharashtra.
5. 5. Turner Prize 2025: Sculptor Nnena Kalu became the first learning-disabled artist to win the prestigious British art prize.
6. 6. India-Netherlands Trade Framework: Both nations established a Joint Trade and Investment Committee (JTIC) to boost co-development in niche technologies.
7. 7. Common Logo for RRBs: Finance Ministry unveiled a new blue and green logo for all Regional Rural Banks in India.
8. 8. Statue of Unity Designer Passes Away: Renowned Indian sculptor Ram Vanji Sutar, designer of the Statue of Unity, died in Noida.
9. 9. PM MITRA Parks Expansion: Greenfield textile parks spanning 1,000+ acres were launched in Lucknow (UP), Kalaburagi (Karnataka), and Navsari (Gujarat).
10. 10. Operation Hawkeye Strike: The United States launched military strikes targeting ISIS strongholds in Syria following threats to American personnel.
11. 11. Bureau of Port Security: Government announced the formation of this statutory body to ensure the safety of vessels and port facilities.
12. 12. Foreign Exchange Reserves Rise: RBI reported India's forex reserves increased by $1.68 billion, reaching a total of $688.94 billion.
13. 13. India's First Gen Z Post Office: Jammu & Kashmir launched its first modernized "Gen Z" post office to enhance public service delivery.
14. 14. Ayush Mark Launched: PM Modi introduced the 'Ayush Mark' as an international quality benchmark for Indian traditional medicine products.
15. 15. Hainan Island Custom Reforms: China separated Hainan Island from its mainland customs zone to create a Hong Kong-style trade hub.
