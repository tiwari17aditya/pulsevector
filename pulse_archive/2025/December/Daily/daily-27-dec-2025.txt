Daily Current Affairs 27 December 2025:-

1. 1. PM Modi chairs 5th National Conference of Chief Secretaries in New Delhi to strengthen Centre-State partnership.
2. 2. President Droupadi Murmu releases the Constitution of India in Santhali language using the Ol Chiki script.
3. 3. India test-fires K-4 nuclear-capable ballistic missile with 3,500 km range from INS Arighaat in Bay of Bengal.
4. 4. North Korea tests new long-range anti-aircraft missile under the personal supervision of leader Kim Jong Un.
5. 5. Russia announces plans to construct a nuclear power station on the Moon by the year 2036.
6. 6. Japan to test-mine rare-earth-rich mud from the deep seabed near Minamitorishima island to secure resources.
7. 7. Ministry of Civil Aviation grants NOCs to three new airlines: Al Hind Air, FlyExpress, and Shankh Air.
8. 8. India’s first indigenous 1.5 Tesla MRI scanner, developed by VoxelGrids, is deployed in Nagpur, Maharashtra.
9. 9. PMGSY completes 25 years, achieving 95% of planned rural road connectivity across India by December 2025.
10. 10. India elected as Chair of the Kimberley Process (KP) for the year 2026, succeeding the UAE.
11. 11. Amit Shah launches India’s first AI-powered Organised Crime Network Database (OCND) developed by NIA and NATGRID.
12. 12. India's largest circular stone labyrinth is discovered in the Solapur district of Maharashtra by archaeologists.
13. 13. IMD issues a Red Alert for dense fog across Bihar, Delhi, Punjab, and Haryana for three days.
14. 14. 359th Birth Anniversary of Sri Guru Gobind Singh Ji (Parkash Utsav) observed with nationwide religious fervor.
15. 15. Vaibhav Suryavanshi, aged 14, is honored with the Pradhan Mantri Rashtriya Bal Puraskar by the President.
