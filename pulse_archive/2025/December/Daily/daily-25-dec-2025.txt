Daily Current Affairs 25 December 2025:-

1. 1. PM Narendra Modi visited Lucknow to honor former PM Atal Bihari Vajpayee’s 101st birth anniversary.
2. 2. India announced a $450 million relief package for Sri Lanka during EAM Jaishankar’s Colombo visit.
3. 3. Government notified 'Petroleum and Natural Gas Rules 2025' to simplify operations and boost energy investments.
4. 4. PM Modi congratulated ISRO for the successful launch of LVM3-M6 carrying the heavy BlueBird Block-2.
5. 5. Union Minister G Kishan Reddy inaugurated India’s first Virtual Reality Mine Simulator (VRMS) in Jharkhand.
6. 6. The 20-MW Dugdha Solar Power Project was inaugurated in Bokaro, Jharkhand, to reduce carbon emissions.
7. 7. Ravi Decee, CEO of DC Books, was conferred France's 'Knight of the Order of Arts and Letters'.
8. 8. Advait Kottary won the Sahitya Akademi Yuva Puraskar 2025 for his English novel on Siddhartha.
9. 9. Jnanpith and Sahitya Akademi awardee Hindi litterateur Vinod Kumar Shukla passed away in Raipur.
10. 10. Jharkhand cabinet approved PESA rules to empower Gram Sabhas in the state's Scheduled Areas.
11. 11. Axis Bank launched 'Digital Merchant Cash Advance Loans' specifically for small MSME merchants.
12. 12. PayNearby secured a Third-Party Application Provider (TPAP) license from NPCI to offer direct UPI services.
13. 13. Virat Kohli and Rohit Sharma both scored centuries for Delhi in the Vijay Hazare Trophy.
14. 14. Alpine SG Pipers, led by Praggnanandhaa, clinched the Global Chess League 2025 title in Mumbai.
15. 15. National Testing Agency (NTA) is considering facial recognition technology to curb impersonation in entrance exams.
