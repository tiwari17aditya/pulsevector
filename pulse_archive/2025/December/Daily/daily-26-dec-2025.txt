Daily Current Affairs 26 December 2025:-

1. 1. India test-fired K-4 nuclear missile from INS Arighaat with 3,500 km range in Bay of Bengal.
2. 2. Government approved three new airlines—Al Hind Air, Fly Express, and Shankh Aviation—to boost domestic aviation.
3. 3. Veer Bal Diwas observed nationwide on 26th December to honor the martyrdom of Sahibzadas.
4. 4. Gujarat achieved 1,879 MW rooftop solar capacity, leading India under PM Surya Ghar Yojna.
5. 5. Delhi Government launched DARPAN 2.0 dashboard for advanced digital governance and real-time performance monitoring.
6. 6. World’s largest open-air theatre festival "Dhanu Yatra" began in Bargarh district of Odisha.
7. 7. Madhya Pradesh to become first state to adopt "Rolling Budget" system starting financial year 2026-27.
8. 8. Union Cabinet approved expansion of Delhi Metro Phase-V A with three new corridors costing ₹12,014 crore.
9. 9. President Droupadi Murmu released the Constitution of India in Santhali language using Ol Chiki script.
10. 10. Indian Coast Guard inducted "Samudra Pratap," the first indigenously built Pollution Control Vessel.
11. 11. Navi Mumbai International Airport started commercial operations with first IndiGo flight landing from Bengaluru.
12. 12. Ministry of Youth Affairs launched comprehensive internship policy with ₹20,000 monthly stipend for sports professionals.
13. 13. India to have 100 "Tsunami Ready" villages, becoming first country in Indian Ocean to achieve this.
14. 14. Sand artist Sudarsan Pattnaik set world record with giant Santa Claus made of sand and apples.
15. 15. Virat Kohli became fastest player to reach 16,000 List A runs during Vijay Hazare Trophy.
