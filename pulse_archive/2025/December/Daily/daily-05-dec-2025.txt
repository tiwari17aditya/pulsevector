Daily Current Affairs 5 December 2025:-

1. 1. GoI approved 5% Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF) blend by 2030 for international flights.
2. 2. The new Prime Minister's Office complex was renamed 'Seva Teerth' by the Government of India.
3. 3. NHAI and Reliance Jio signed an MoU for telecom-based safety alerts across National Highways.
4. 4. The Sahkar Taxi Cooperative began the soft launch of its ride-hailing service 'Bharat Taxi' in Delhi and Gujarat.
5. 5. Odisha CM Mohan Charan Majhi inaugurated the 15th International Sand Art Festival and 36th Konark Festival.
6. 6. IGI Airport (Delhi), managed by DIAL, became India's first high-capacity airport certified Water-Positive.
7. 7. Stefan Priesner was appointed as the UN Resident Coordinator in India by UN Secretary-General Antonio Guterres.
8. 8. The tenure of ONGC Chairman Arun Kumar Singh was extended for one year till December 6, 2026.
9. 9. The theme for World Soil Day 2025 (observed on December 5) is "Healthy Soils for Healthy Cities".
10. 10. The theme for International Volunteer Day 2025 (observed on December 5) is “Every Contribution Matters.”
11. 11. Uttarakhand CM Pushkar Dhami felicitated women scientists with the 'Young Women Scientists Achievement Award-2025'.
12. 12. The 6th observance of the International Day of Banks 2025 (December 4) had the theme “Banks Powering a Livable Planet”.
13. 13. The new seismic map using PEMA methodology revealed that 75% of the Indian population lives in seismically active regions.
14. 14. Hungarian writer László Krasznahorkai won the 2025 Nobel Prize for Literature.
15. 15. The Lok Sabha approved the Central Excise (Amendment) Bill, 2025.
