Weekly Current Affairs ( 8 December - 14 December )

1. 1. India hosted the 20th session of UNESCO's Intergovernmental Committee in New Delhi.
2. 2. The RBI released its 5th Bi-monthly Monetary Policy for FY26; the repo rate remained unchanged.
3. 3. The 23rd India-Russia Annual Summit was held in New Delhi, celebrating 25 years of partnership.
4. 4. India and Russia adopted 'Programme 2030' to diversify trade beyond defense and energy.
5. 5. The India-Malaysia joint military exercise 'Harimau Shakti 2025' commenced in Rajasthan.
6. 6. DRDO handed over seven indigenous technologies developed under the TDF scheme to the Indian Armed Forces.
7. 7. Arjun Erigaisi won the Jerusalem Masters 2025 chess tournament, defeating Viswanathan Anand.
8. 8. Simone Tata, former Chairperson of Lakme and founder of Trent Limited, passed away.
9. 9. Siliserh Lake (Rajasthan) and Kopra Jalashay (Chhattisgarh) were designated as new Ramsar Sites.
10. 10. The Ratle Hydroelectric Project, 850 MW, is being built on the Chenab River in Jammu & Kashmir.
11. 11. The Supreme Court issued key directives for ensuring accessibility for Persons with Disabilities (PwDs) in prisons.
12. 12. The Indian government is considering the introduction of a 'Right to Disconnect' rule for employees.
13. 13. The Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) restricted regulated entities from associating with unregistered finfluencers.
14. 14. The Development of Pharmaceuticals and Medical Devices Bureau (DPMB) launched a new scheme for promoting medical tourism.
15. 15. The Seventh UN Environment Assembly (UNEA-7) was held in Nairobi, focusing on the Medium-Term Strategy.
16. 16. Astronomers observed the Geminid Meteor Shower, which originates from Asteroid 3200 Phaethon.
17. 17. Ponduru Khadi from Andhra Pradesh, known for manual production, is seeking a GI Tag renewal.
18. 18. Ponduru Khadi uses three types of indigenous cotton: hill cotton, punasa cotton, or red cotton.
19. 19. Tapanuli Orangutan (Pongo tapanuliensis), the most endangered great ape, is Critically Endangered.
20. 20. The first successful ascent of Mount Kangto (7,042 metres) was achieved by an Indian Army team.
21. 21. LIC and Yes Bank announced a Bancassurance Partnership to expand insurance access across the country.
22. 22. The 200th anniversary of Dadabhai Naoroji, the 'Grand Old Man of India', was observed.
23. 23. Dadabhai Naoroji is famous for the 'Drain of Wealth Theory' against British rule.
24. 24. India's Foreign Reserves touched $687.26 Billion in December 2025, according to latest data.
25. 25. The Supreme Court mandated mandatory mental-health infrastructure in all schools with over 100 students.
26. 26. The Centre for Disease Control (CDC) issued a global alert on a rising drug-resistant Gonorrhoea strain.
27. 27. December 10 was observed as Human Rights Day, commemorating the UDHR adoption.
28. 28. December 11 was observed as International Mountain Day and UNICEF Day.
29. 29. December 12 was observed as Universal Health Coverage Day worldwide.
30. 30. December 14 was observed as National Energy Conservation Day in India.
