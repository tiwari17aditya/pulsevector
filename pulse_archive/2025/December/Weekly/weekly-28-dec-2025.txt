Weekly Current Affairs (( 22 Dec 2025 ] - [ 28 Dec 2025 ] )

1. 1. National Mathematics Day observed on December 22 to honor Srinivasa Ramanujan’s 138th birth anniversary.
2. 2. India successfully test-fired 3,500-km range K-4 intermediate-range ballistic missile from nuclear submarine INS Arighaat.
3. 3. Government approved three new airlines: Al Hind Air, Fly Express, and Shankh Aviation for India.
4. 4. Veer Bal Diwas observed on December 26 to honor the martyrdom of Guru Gobind Singh's sons.
5. 5. Good Governance Day celebrated on December 25, marking Atal Bihari Vajpayee’s 101st birth anniversary.
6. 6. Prime Minister Rashtriya Bal Puraskar 2025 recognized 20 young achievers for their exceptional contributions.
7. 7. Gujarat reached 1,879 MW rooftop solar capacity, leading India under PM Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojna.
8. 8. Delhi launched DARPAN 2.0 dashboard for real-time monitoring of government projects and departmental performance.
9. 9. Odisha’s Bargarh hosted Dhanu Yatra, the world’s largest open-air theatre festival based on Krishna's life.
10. 10. Hockey vice-captain Hardik Singh was nominated for the Major Dhyan Chand Khel Ratna Award 2025.
11. 11. East Bengal FC triumphed in the inaugural SAFF Women’s Club Championship 2025 held this week.
12. 12. India Women’s cricket team defeated Sri Lanka by 8 wickets in the T20I series opener.
13. 13. Alpine SG Pipers emerged as champions of the 3rd Global Chess League 2025 in Mumbai.
14. 14. Supercomputer PARAM Rudra was unveiled at IIT-Patna to strengthen high-performance computing in India.
15. 15. PM Modi received Ethiopia’s highest honor, 'Great Honor Nishan of Ethiopia', for strengthening bilateral ties.
16. 16. India’s first PPP-model medical colleges established in tribal-dominated districts of Madhya Pradesh this week.
17. 17. Unit-2 of the 2,000 MW Subansiri Hydroelectric Project started commercial operations in Northeast India.
18. 18. Union Cabinet approved Delhi Metro Phase-VA expansion featuring three new high-capacity corridors for commuters.
19. 19. India and Netherlands signed an MoU to boost cooperation in trade, investment, and sustainability sectors.
20. 20. Michaela Benthos made history by becoming the world’s first wheelchair user to travel to space.
21. 21. Shashwat Sharma was officially appointed as the new Managing Director and CEO of Airtel India.
22. 22. Uttar Pradesh approved the establishment of its first forest university to promote environmental research.
23. 23. Australia declared December 21 as the “Day of Reflection” to honor national history and culture.
24. 24. Uttar Pradesh announced Road Safety Month to be observed from January 1 to 31, 2026.
25. 25. Tata Advanced Systems delivered the first batch of indigenous WhAP 8x8 armoured vehicles to Morocco.
26. 26. India was re-elected to the UNESCO Executive Board for the 2025–2029 term with high votes.
27. 27. President Droupadi Murmu gave assent to the Viksit Bharat G RAM G Bill, replacing MGNREGA.
28. 28. PM Modi inaugurated the 60th All India DGP–IGP Conference 2025 in Naya Raipur, Chhattisgarh.
29. 29. Indian Women’s hockey team defeated South Africa to win the T20I series 3-1 this week.
30. 30. Oxford University Press officially named “Rage Bait” as the Word of the Year for 2025.
