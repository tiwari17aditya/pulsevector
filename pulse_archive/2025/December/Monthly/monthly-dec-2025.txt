title :- Monthly Current Affairs - ( December,2025 )

1. 1. India surpassed Japan to become the world’s fourth-largest economy with a GDP of $4.18 trillion.
2. 2. Former Bangladesh Prime Minister and BNP Chairperson Khaleda Zia passed away in Dhaka at age 80.
3. 3. DRDO successfully conducted the maiden flight test of Pinaka Long Range Guided Rocket (LRGR-120) in Odisha.
4. 4. US President-elect Donald Trump was awarded the Israel Peace Prize following the Florida talks at Mar-a-Lago.
5. 5. Finance Ministry notified 100% Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) rules for the insurance sector to boost reforms.
6. 6. India added 5 new unicorns in 2025, including Navi, Jumbotail, and Juspay, reaching 124 total unicorns.
7. 7. Supreme Court ruled that the right to a passport is part of personal liberty under Article 21.
8. 8. Deepti Sharma became the highest wicket-taker in Women’s T20 International cricket history.
9. 9. Bureau of Indian Standards notified IS 19445:2025, India’s first dedicated standard for bomb disposal systems.
10. 10. India achieved a landmark by having over 100 UNESCO-recognized "Tsunami Ready" villages in the Indian Ocean.
11. 11. Bihar’s first Param Rudra supercomputer was inaugurated at IIT Patna under the National Supercomputing Mission.
12. 12. Deepavali festival was officially inscribed on the UNESCO Intangible Cultural Heritage List for 2025.
13. 13. India and Oman signed a Comprehensive Economic Partnership Agreement (CEPA) to enhance bilateral trade.
14. 14. Department of Telecommunications released National Frequency Allocation Plan 2025 to support 6G and satellite services.
15. 15. Indian squash team won its maiden World Cup title in Chennai, defeating top-seeded Hong Kong.
16. 16. Union Health Ministry banned oral formulations of Nimesulide exceeding 100 mg due to safety concerns.
17. 17. Arjun Erigaisi and Koneru Humpy won bronze medals at the 2025 FIDE World Rapid Championships.
18. 18. Indian Army performed the nation's first 3D Flex Aqueous Angiography for advanced glaucoma care.
19. 19. Jayshree Ullal topped the Hurun India Rich List 2025 among Indian-origin tech executives.
20. 20. Himachal Pradesh launched the ‘Green to Gold’ initiative to promote regulated industrial hemp cultivation.
21. 21. India imposed a three-year safeguard duty on steel imports to protect domestic manufacturers from low-priced shipments.
22. 22. Government approved the REPM scheme with ₹7,000 crore to build a rare-earth permanent magnet ecosystem.
23. 23. ISRO successfully ground-tested an upgraded third stage of the Small Satellite Launch Vehicle (SSLV).
24. 24. India and Australia announced zero-duty access for 100% of Indian exports effective from January 2026.
25. 25. Union Minister Amit Shah inaugurated the redeveloped ‘Batadrava Than’ in Assam, birthplace of Srimanta Sankardeva.
26. 26. National Highway 45 (NH-45) introduced India’s first wildlife-safe road features with ‘table-top red markings’.
27. 27. RBI report confirmed Gross Non-Performing Assets (GNPA) fell to a multi-decadal low of 2.1%.
28. 28. Prime Minister Narendra Modi received Ethiopia’s highest national award, "The Great Honour Nishan of Ethiopia."
29. 29. India developed "PathGennie," an open-source tool to accelerate drug research and biomedical studies.
30. 30. Rajasthan became the first Indian state to fully digitize voter rolls under Special Intensive Revision.
31. 31. Indian AI Research Organisation (IAIRO) was approved to be established in GIFT City, Gujarat.
32. 32. Department of Telecommunications reduced designation application fees by 50% for telecom startups and women-owned firms.
33. 33. India recorded over 5.5 lakh trademark registrations in 2024-25, the highest-ever milestone for innovation.
34. 34. Ministry of Mines entered bilateral agreements with Zambia and Argentina for critical mineral supply chains.
35. 35. National Rabies Control Programme highlighted that India accounts for 36% of global rabies deaths.
36. 36. Syria unveiled new banknotes removing two zeros, with circulation set to begin in January 2026.
37. 37. China set a world record by propelling a maglev train to 700 kmph in two seconds.
38. 38. Gujarat was selected to host eight disciplines of the World Police and Fire Games 2029.
39. 39. Union Government launched a ₹4,531-crore Market Access Support (MAS) scheme to strengthen export ecosystems.
40. 40. Indian Army successfully tested the Pralay tactical missile in a salvo launch off the Odisha coast.
41. 41. 8th Pay Commission implementation was confirmed to be effective from January 1, 2026.
42. 42. PV Sindhu was elected as the Chairperson of the BWF Athletes’ Commission for 2026–2029.
43. 43. Tracxn report showed Indian startups take an average of 5.8 years to reach unicorn status.
44. 44. UNESCO recognized Italian national cuisine as part of the Intangible Cultural Heritage list.
45. 45. German engineer Michaela Benthaus was announced as the world’s first wheelchair user to travel to space.
46. 46. Ministry of Development of North-East Region registered 150 new Geographical Indication (GI) products.
47. 47. RBI introduced the "FREE-AI" Framework to ensure ethical and transparent AI use in banking.
48. 48. Smriti Mandhana joined the elite club of players scoring 10,000 runs in women’s international cricket.
49. 49. Digital payments reached a record 97.6% of retail transaction value in India during late 2025.
50. 50. India’s clean energy capacity saw significant gains, though structural reforms remain key for self-reliance.
