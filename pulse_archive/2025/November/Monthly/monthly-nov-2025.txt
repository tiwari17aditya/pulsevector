Monthly Current Affairs - ( November , 2025 )

1. 1. Justice Surya Kant was appointed as the 53rd Chief Justice of India in November 2025.
2. 2. Bihar State Assembly Elections 2025 saw the National Democratic Alliance (NDA) win 202 seats out of 243.
3. 3. Nitish Kumar was sworn in as the new Chief Minister of Bihar following the state elections.
4. 4. The Hornbill Festival 2025 in Nagaland had the UK, Ireland, and Switzerland as its country partners.
5. 5. India's GDP grew by 8.2% in the second quarter of the financial year 2025-26, surpassing forecasts.
6. 6. The IMF projected India's economy to grow at 6.6% in FY 2025–26 in its Article IV Consultation report.
7. 7. Moody's Ratings projected India's economy to lead emerging markets with 7% GDP growth in 2025.
8. 8. PM Modi virtually inaugurated Safran Aircraft Engine Services India (SAESI), India's first global MRO facility.
9. 9. India's first Indigenous Vessel Traffic Management System (VTMS) was deployed at Vizhinjam Seaport, Kerala.
10. 10. The government proposed the Constitution 131st Amendment Bill to include Chandigarh under Article 240.
11. 11. Nepal's new Rs 100 banknote featured the Indian territories of Kalapani, Lipulekh, and Limpiyadhura.
12. 12. Operation Sagar Bandhu was launched by India to deliver relief materials to cyclone-hit Sri Lanka.
13. 13. India and Afghanistan agreed to create a bilateral Joint Chamber of Commerce and Industry.
14. 14. PM Modi ceremonially hoisted the saffron flag (Dharma Dhwaj) at the Ram Temple in Ayodhya.
15. 15. Dharmendra was remembered and honoured in two emotional prayer meets by family, friends, and fans.
16. 16. The 44th India International Trade Fair (IITF) was held in New Delhi under the theme 'Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat'.
17. 17. The Basic Animal Husbandry Statistics 2025 report was released by the Department of Animal Husbandry & Dairying.
18. 18. Andhra Pradesh topped the list of states in Egg Production with an 18.37% share of the total production.
19. 19. India won the ICC Women's World Cup 2025 for the first time, defeating South Africa.
20. 20. Deepti Sharma was named the Player of the Series in the ICC Women's World Cup 2025.
21. 21. Raahul VS became India's 91st Chess Grandmaster after his win at the 6th ASEAN Individual Championship.
22. 22. Tennis legend Roger Federer (Switzerland) was inducted into the International Tennis Hall of Fame.
23. 23. Rohit Sharma was named the Tournament Ambassador for the ICC Men's T20 World Cup 2026.
24. 24. David Szalay (Hungarian-British author) won the Booker Prize 2025 for his novel "Flesh".
25. 25. Fatima Bosch from Mexico was crowned Miss Universe 2025 at the 74th edition in Thailand.
26. 26. "Parasocial" was named the Cambridge Dictionary's Word of the Year 2025.
27. 27. Ravindra Jadeja joined the Elite 4000 Runs–300 Wickets Club in international cricket.
28. 28. Max Verstappen won the 2025 Las Vegas Grand Prix in Formula 1 racing.
29. 29. India sealed a ₹7,995-crore deal with the U.S. for support of the MH-60R Seahawk helicopters.
30. 30. The National Mission for Mentoring (NMM) modalities were assigned to the National Council for Teacher Education (NCTE).
31. 31. The Mudh-Nyoma Air Force Station was inaugurated in Ladakh at an altitude of 13,700 feet.
32. 32. The International Institute for Democracy and Electoral Assistance (IDEA) will be chaired by CEC Gyanesh Kumar in 2026.
33. 33. INS Mahe, the first of the indigenously built ASW-SWC vessels, was commissioned by the Indian Navy.
34. 34. The Higher Education Commission of India Bill 2025 seeks to merge UGC, NCTE, and AICTE.
35. 35. The IMF retained its C-grade for India's national accounts statistics, citing "some shortcomings."
36. 36. Constitution Day (Samvidhan Diwas) was celebrated across India on November 26.
37. 37. International Day for the Elimination of Violence against Women was observed on November 25.
38. 38. World Diabetes Day and Children's Day (Nehru Jayanti) were both observed on November 14.
39. 39. World Science Day for Peace and Development was observed globally on November 10.
40. 40. National Education Day was celebrated on November 11, marking Maulana Abul Kalam Azad's birth.
41. 41. World Tsunami Awareness Day was observed internationally on November 5.
42. 42. World Vegan Day and the formation days of several Indian states were marked on November 1.
43. 43. Gogabeel Lake in Bihar was declared India's newest Ramsar Site, an ox-bow lake.
44. 44. Oman joined the UNESCO's Man and the Biosphere (MAB) Council for the 2025-29 term.
45. 45. The Colombo Security Conclave inducted Seychelles as its newest member state.
46. 46. The Golden Jackal's conservation status remains Least Concern globally.
47. 47. MATSYA 6000 is a manned submersible developed by NIOT under the Samudrayaan mission.
48. 48. National Newborn Week 2025 was observed from November 15-21.
49. 49. Russia's President Vladimir Putin is scheduled to visit India on December 4–5.
50. 50. Tesla opened its largest all-in-one centre in Gurugram, India.
