title :- Daily Current Affairs 14 Nov 2025:-

1. 1. Children's Day was observed in India on November 14 to commemorate the birth anniversary of Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru, the nation's first Prime Minister.
2. 2. The Directorate of Revenue Intelligence (DRI) launched "Operation Bullion Blaze," leading to the seizure of 11.88 kg of smuggled gold in Mumbai, valued at approximately ₹15.05 crore.
3. 3. The Global TB Report 2025, released by the WHO, found that India has the highest global burden of tuberculosis (TB) cases, accounting for 25% of all cases worldwide.
4. 4. The Border Roads Organisation (BRO) constructed the strategic Nyoma Airbase in Ladakh near the Line of Actual Control (LAC), designed to handle heavy aircraft and fighter operations.
5. 5. India and Nepal signed an amendment to the Treaty of Transit to facilitate the movement of rail-based freight between Jogbani (India) and Biratnagar (Nepal).
6. 6. The Union Cabinet approved a rationalisation of royalty rates for four critical minerals: graphite, caesium, rubidium, and zirconium, to boost domestic mining.
7. 7. The Border Security Force (BSF) commissioned its first all-women drone operations unit named 'Durga Drone Squadron' at the School of Drone Warfare in Gwalior, Madhya Pradesh.
8. 8. Punjab became the first state in India to fully implement the amended BharatNet Scheme, providing high-speed broadband connectivity to all Gram Panchayats.
9. 9. Adani Ports and Special Economic Zone Limited (APSEZ) joined the Taskforce on Nature-related Financial Disclosures (TNFD) as an "Adopter," pledging to begin nature-related reporting from FY26.
10. 10. Researchers from IIT Bombay developed India's first indigenous Quantum Diamond Microscope (QDM) under the National Quantum Mission for nanoscale dynamic magnetic field imaging.
11. 11. Blue Origin successfully launched NASA's ESCAPADE (Escape and Plasma Acceleration and Dynamics Explorers) mission, the first coordinated multi-spacecraft orbital science mission to Mars.
12. 12. The Cabinet extended additional credit facilities and approved a six-year Export Promotion Mission (EPM) with an outlay of ₹45,060 crore to boost the export sector.
13. 13. Moody's Ratings released its Macro 2026-27 Outlook Report, projecting India as the fastest-growing G-20 economy through 2027, with 7% GDP growth in 2025.
14. 14. The Union Cabinet approved the launch of the Credit Guarantee Scheme for Exporters (CGSE) with a coverage of up to ₹20,000 crore to ease liquidity pressures.
15. 15. The Haryana State Women's Commission appointed cricketer Shafali Verma as its Brand Ambassador following her World Cup-winning performance.
