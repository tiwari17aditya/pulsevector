Daily Current Affairs November 3, 2025

1. 1. Kerala Declared Extreme Poverty-Free :- Kerala became the first Indian state to officially declare itself free from extreme poverty, following the successful implementation of its Extreme Poverty Eradication Programme (EPEP).
2. 2. India's LVM3 Launches GSAT-7R :- ISRO successfully launched the GSAT-7R communication satellite using its heavy-lift rocket LVM3 (Launch Vehicle Mark 3), showcasing India's independent capability for launching satellites exceeding 4,000 kg into Geosynchronous Transfer Orbit (GTO).
3. 3. Exercise 'Poorvi Prachand Prahar' :- The Indian Armed Forces announced the staging of the tri-service military exercise 'Poorvi Prachand Prahar' in Mechuka, Arunachal Pradesh, designed to validate multi-domain integration across land, air, and maritime fronts.
4. 4. ICC Women's World Cup 2025 Won by India :- The Indian Women's Cricket Team clinched their first-ever ICC Women's Cricket World Cup title by defeating South Africa by 52 runs in the final held in Navi Mumbai.
5. 5. BCCI Announces Cash Reward :- The BCCI announced a substantial ₹51 crore cash reward for the Women's Cricket Team following their historic victory in the ICC Women's World Cup 2025.
6. 6. Manipur Audiotapes Found 'Tampered' :- The Supreme Court was informed that a forensic report found whistleblower audiotapes, allegedly containing conversations of a former Manipur Chief Minister, to be "modified, edited and tampered."
7. 7. Tragic Accident in Telangana :- Nineteen individuals were killed, including a three-month-old girl, in a tragic road accident in Telangana's Rangareddy district when a lorry collided head-on with a public transport bus.
8. 8. Digital Arrest Scams Challenge :- The Supreme Court highlighted that 'Digital Arrest' scams pose a "very big challenge," with more than ₹3,000 crore having been lost by victims in India, mostly from the elderly population.
9. 9. Abu Dhabi Hosts Oil Summit :- Abu Dhabi hosted a major oil summit as OPEC+ announced a halt to production hikes planned for the first quarter of 2026.
10. 10. Afghanistan Earthquake :- A strong earthquake in Afghanistan's Hindu Kush region resulted in at least 20 deaths and over 300 injuries, according to the Health Ministry.
11. 11. Russia Launches Khabarovsk Submarine :- Russia launched its nuclear-powered submarine 'Khabarovsk', built to deploy the Poseidon nuclear-capable underwater drone.
12. 12. DDA to Launch First TOD :- The Delhi Development Authority (DDA) is set to launch Delhi's first Transit-Oriented Development (TOD) in the East Delhi Hub in Karkardooma.
13. 13. India's First AI Policy Framework :- India announced its first comprehensive Artificial Intelligence (AI) Policy Framework, focusing on ethical development, public use, and regulatory guidelines for the sector.
14. 14. India Maritime Week Conclave :- The India Maritime Week 2025 event concluded in Mumbai, with the logo of the proposed National Maritime Heritage Complex (NMHC) at Lothal, Gujarat, being unveiled.
15. 15. Passenger Train Derailment in UK :- A passenger train travelling from Glasgow Central to London Euston derailed after striking a landslip near Shap, Cumbria, due to heavy rainfall, resulting in minor injuries.
