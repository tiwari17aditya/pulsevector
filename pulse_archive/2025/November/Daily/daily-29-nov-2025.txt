Daily Current Affairs 29 Nov 2025:-

1. 1. India's Q2 FY26 GDP growth surged to a robust 8.2%, driven by manufacturing and services.
2. 2. Russian President Vladimir Putin is scheduled to visit India on December 4–5 for the 23rd India–Russia Annual Summit.
3. 3. India signed a ₹7,995 crore agreement with the US for a follow-on support package for the Navy's MH-60R Seahawk fleet.
4. 4. Andhra Pradesh approved the creation of three new districts: Polavaram, Markapuram, and Madanapalle, raising the total to 29.
5. 5. Rohit Sharma was appointed as the tournament ambassador for the ICC Men's T20 World Cup 2026, to be hosted by India and Sri Lanka.
6. 6. The World Telecommunication Development Conference 2025 concluded in Baku, focusing on charting future global telecom development.
7. 7. Nepal Rastra Bank released a new ₹100 note featuring a revised map that includes three Indian territories: Kalapani, Lipulekh, and Limpiyadhura.
8. 8. The archaeological town of Sirpur in Chhattisgarh is undergoing preparations for a UNESCO World Heritage nomination.
9. 9. Cyclone Ditwah neared the Tamil Nadu coast, prompting the IMD to warn of heavy rain in Tamil Nadu, Puducherry, and South Andhra Pradesh.
10. 10. The annual 'Basic Animal Husbandry Statistics (BAHS-2025)' was released by the Union Minister of State for Fisheries, Animal Husbandry & Dairying.
11. 11. India's first Indigenous Vessel Traffic Management System (VTMS) was developed by NTCPWC, IIT Madras, and deployed at Vizhinjam Port in Kerala.
12. 12. Scientists developed 'OncoMark,' an AI framework for personalized cancer therapy, analyzing cancer's molecular hallmarks.
13. 13. India and Afghanistan agreed to create a bilateral Joint Chamber of Commerce and Industry to boost trade ties.
14. 14. International Jaguar Day is observed annually on November 29 to raise awareness about the protection of Jaguars and their habitats.
15. 15. The Union Cabinet approved Phase-2 of the Pune Metro Rail Project, covering an additional 31.636 km.
