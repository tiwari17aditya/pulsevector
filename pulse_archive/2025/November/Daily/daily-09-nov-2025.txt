Daily Current Affairs November 9, 2025:-

1. 1. Sri Sri Ravi Shankar Award: Sri Sri Ravi Shankar was awarded the 2025 World Leader for Peace and Security Award by the Boston Global Forum (BGF) and the AI World Society (AIWS) for his contributions to humanitarian service, reconciliation, and peace-building.
2. 2. Uttarakhand's Silver Jubilee & Projects: Prime Minister Narendra Modi attended the Silver Jubilee celebrations of Uttarakhand's formation (November 9th) and launched and laid the foundation stone for development projects worth over ₹8,000 crore in Dehradun, covering sectors like technical education, water, and urban development.
3. 3. National Legal Services Day: National Legal Services Day was observed on November 9, 2025, commemorating the enactment of the Legal Services Authorities Act, 1987, which came into force on November 9, 1995, and aims to ensure free and fair legal aid for all.
4. 4. Assam Cabinet Approves Anti-Polygamy Bill: The Assam Cabinet approved a bill to prohibit polygamy, classifying it as a cognisable offense and making the accused ineligible for immediate bail, with provisions for compensation for victims.
5. 5. India's First Individual Pistol World Championships Medal: Shooter Anish Bhanwala won the silver medal in the men's 25m rapid fire pistol event at the ISSF World Championships in Cairo, becoming the first Indian individual pistol shooter to win a World Championships medal in an Olympic event.
6. 6. SBI's Historic Dual Milestone: The State Bank of India (SBI) achieved a historic dual milestone by crossing $100 billion in market capitalization and ₹100 trillion in total business (advances and deposits), becoming the first Indian bank to reach this scale.
7. 7. Indigenous CAR T-Cell Therapy Launched: PM Modi launched NexCAR19, India's first homegrown CAR T-cell therapy developed by IIT Bombay's startup ImmunoACT and Tata Memorial Hospital, to treat B-cell leukemia and lymphoma.
8. 8. COP30 Inaugurated: COP30 (30th Conference of the Parties to the UNFCCC) was inaugurated in Belém, Brazil, with a focus on adaptation measures and the launch of the Tropical Forests Forever Facility (TFFF), which India has joined as an observer.
9. 9. India-Angola Defence & Energy Cooperation: India is finalising a $200 million Line of Credit to assist Angola's defence modernisation, strengthening cooperation in defence and energy sectors.
10. 10. China Commissions Aircraft Carrier: China officially commissioned its third aircraft carrier, 'Fujian,' which features an Electromagnetic Aircraft Launch System (EMALS), marking a major step in its naval modernization.
11. 11. New Gram Sabha Initiative: The Ministry of Panchayati Raj is set to launch an initiative where a mock Gram Sabha will be conducted in identified schools, with students from classes 9-12 playing the roles of village officials.
12. 12. Bhanwala's Shooting Success: Anish Bhanwala's silver medal at the ISSF World Championships is significant as it secures a major medal in a rapid-fire pistol event, enhancing India's presence in international shooting.
13. 13. Ramnami Tribe of Chhattisgarh: The Ramnami tribe originates from Chhattisgarh and is known for its unique devotional practice of tattooing the name "Ram" on their bodies and homes.
14. 14. TEC-IIT Bombay MoU: An MoU was signed between the Telecommunication Engineering Centre (TEC) and IIT Bombay for joint research in advanced telecom technologies and standardization.
15. 15. Aditya-L1 Collaboration: Scientists from the Indian Institute of Astrophysics (IIA) and NASA collaborated using the VELC payload on Aditya-L1 to record the very first spectroscopic observations of a Coronal Mass Ejection (CME) in the visible wavelength range close to the Sun.
