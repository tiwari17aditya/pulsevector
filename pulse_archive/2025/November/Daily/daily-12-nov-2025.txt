Daily Current Affairs 12 Nov 2025:-

1. 1. World Pneumonia Day was observed globally on November 12 to raise awareness about the infectious disease and mobilize action against it.
2. 2. India launched the National Green Hydrogen Policy 2025, aiming to reduce carbon dependency by 45% before 2030 and encourage private investment in renewable fuel projects.
3. 3. NPCI International Payments Limited (NIPL) and Bahrain's BENEFIT company partnered to enable real-time cross-border remittances between India's UPI system and Bahrain's Fawri+ network.
4. 4. The 6th India-Vietnam Army Exercise VINBAX commenced in Hanoi, Vietnam, to strengthen defence cooperation and interoperability for UN peacekeeping capabilities.
5. 5. PM Modi inaugurated the 1,020 MW Punatsangchhu-II Hydroelectric Project during his official state visit to Bhutan, a project fully financed by the Government of India.
6. 6. The Supreme Court of India directed the Union Government to disclose detailed data on political funding under the Electoral Bond scheme to enhance transparency.
7. 7. India unveiled its first MWh-scale Vanadium Redox Flow Battery (VRFB) at NTPC NETRA, Noida, a 3 MWh system for long-duration energy storage.
8. 8. Tata Power announced plans to set up India's largest solar wafers and ingots plant with a 10 GW capacity to complete its solar manufacturing chain and reduce import dependence.
9. 9. The National Investigation Agency (NIA) officially took charge of the probe into the car explosion near Delhi's Red Fort that was initially reported on November 11.
10. 10. The 11th edition of the India–Sri Lanka Joint Military Exercise 'Mitra Shakti' commenced at the Foreign Training Node, Belagavi, Karnataka, and will continue till November 23.
11. 11. The CAG of India approved the creation of two specialised cadres: the Central Revenue Audit and Central Expenditure Audit cadres under the Indian Audit & Accounts Department (IA&AD).
12. 12. Ethiopia is set to host the 32nd Conference of the Parties (COP32) to the UNFCCC in 2027, marking the first time a country from East Africa will host the global climate summit.
13. 13. UNESCO released the world's first global ethical framework on neurotechnology, the “Recommendation on the Ethics of Neurotechnology,” effective from November 12, 2025.
14. 14. The Ministry of Statistics & Programme Implementation (MoSPI) announced a base revision of the Index of Industrial Production (IIP), updating the base year to 2022–23.
15. 15. The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) maintained the repo rate at 6.25%, signaling confidence in moderating inflation trends despite global economic fluctuations.
