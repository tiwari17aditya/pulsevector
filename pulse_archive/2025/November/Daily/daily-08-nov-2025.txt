Daily Current Affairs November 8, 2025:-

1. 1. Khangchendzonga National Park Rated 'Good' by IUCN:- India's Khangchendzonga National Park, a UNESCO 'mixed' World Heritage Site in Sikkim, has been rated "Good" in the IUCN World Heritage Outlook 2025 assessment for effective conservation.
2. 2. India's First Homegrown CAR T-Cell Therapy Launched:- Prime Minister Narendra Modi launched NexCAR19, India's first indigenously developed CAR T-cell therapy for B-cell leukemia and lymphoma, developed by ImmunoACT (an IIT Bombay incubated startup) in collaboration with Tata Memorial Hospital.
3. 3. Four New Vande Bharat Express Trains Flagged Off:- PM Modi flagged off four new Vande Bharat Express services from Varanasi, enhancing connectivity on the routes: Banaras–Khajuraho, Lucknow Junction–Saharanpur, Firozpur Cantt–Delhi, and Ernakulam–Bengaluru.
4. 4. World Radiography Day 2025:- World Radiography Day was observed on November 8, 2025, with the theme "Radiographers: Seeing the Unseen"; the day commemorates Wilhelm Roentgen's discovery of the X-ray in 1895.
5. 5. President Murmu's State Visit to Angola and Botswana:- President Droupadi Murmu began a state visit to Angola and Botswana, commencing her Angola visit on November 8, 2025, to boost India's engagement with the African continent.
6. 6. India’s 91st Chess Grandmaster:- Raahul VS has become India's 91st Chess Grandmaster (GM) after his performance at the 6th ASEAN Individual Championship 2025.
7. 7. SBI Crosses $100 Billion Market Cap:- State Bank of India (SBI) became the first Indian public sector bank and the sixth Indian company overall to cross a market capitalization of $100 billion and ₹100 trillion in total business.
8. 8. Karnataka First State with Paid Menstrual Leave:- Karnataka became the first state in India to grant 12 days of paid menstrual leave annually for women employees across the government and private sectors.
9. 9. James D. Watson Passes Away:- American molecular biologist and Nobel laureate James D. Watson, who co-discovered the structure of the DNA double helix (along with Francis Crick in 1953), passed away at the age of 97.
10. 10. Denmark to Ban Social Media for Under-15s:- The government of Denmark announced plans to ban social media access for children under the age of 15, citing concerns over mental health and data privacy.
11. 11. National Urban Conclave 2025:- The two-day National Urban Conclave was organized at Yashobhoomi, New Delhi, focusing on the theme "Sustainable Urban Development and Governance."
12. 12. Dumpsite Remediation Accelerator Programme (DRAP) Launched:- Union Minister for Housing and Urban Affairs, Shri Manohar Lal, launched the DRAP during the National Urban Conclave to fast-track the remediation of dumpsites across urban India, aiming for "Lakshya Zero Dumpsites" by September 2026.
13. 13. Vande Mataram 150th Anniversary Commemoration:- PM Modi inaugurated the year-long commemoration of the 150th anniversary of the national song "Vande Mataram" (composed by Bankim Chandra Chatterji in 1875).
14. 14. BIMSTEC-India Ocean Research Network Conference:- The first BIMSTEC-India Ocean Research Network Conference was held in Kochi, Kerala, to strengthen blue economy cooperation among BIMSTEC member nations.
15. 15. SEBI on Trading Apps:- SEBI directed platforms to fast-track mandatory advertiser verification in the securities market and recommended a distinct verification label for registered intermediary trading apps.
