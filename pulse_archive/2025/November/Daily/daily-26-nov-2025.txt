Daily Current Affairs 26 nov 2025:-

1. 1. India celebrates Constitution Day on November 26, marking the adoption of the Constitution in 1949.
2. 2. Tributes paid to martyrs on the 17th anniversary of the 26/11 Mumbai Terror Attack of 2008.
3. 3. PM Modi virtually inaugurated the Safran Aircraft Engine Services India (SAESI) facility in Hyderabad.
4. 4. Union MoS Dr. Jitendra Singh announced a ₹720 Crore Quantum Fabrication & Central Facilities at IIT Bombay.
5. 5. UIDAI deactivated over 2 crore Aadhaar numbers of deceased individuals as a database clean-up effort.
6. 6. Centre approved the Rare Earth Magnet Initiative with a ₹7,280 crore financial outlay for manufacturing.
7. 7. India-Nepal Joint Military Exercise SURYAKIRAN-XIX began in Pithoragarh, Uttarakhand.
8. 8. Rohit Sharma was appointed the Tournament Ambassador for the ICC Men's T20 World Cup 2026.
9. 9. Indian Navy's indigenous aircraft carrier INS Vikrant arrived at the Port of Colombo for the International Fleet Review.
10. 10. MoSJE and MoRD signed an MoU to integrate 5000 De-Notified, Nomadic and Semi-Nomadic (DNT) SHG's into DAY-NRLM.
11. 11. Indonesia was officially declared Polio-free by the World Health Organization (WHO).
12. 12. RBI announced the interlinking of UPI (India) with TIPS (Eurosystem) for faster cross-border remittances.
13. 13. Brazil's former President Jair Bolsonaro was sentenced to 27 years in prison for plotting a military coup.
14. 14. Gavi and UNICEF secured an affordable malaria vaccine deal to increase access in low-income countries.
15. 15. Andhra Pradesh approved the creation of three additional districts, increasing the total to 29.
