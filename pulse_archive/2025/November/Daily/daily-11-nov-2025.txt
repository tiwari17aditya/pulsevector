Daily Current Affairs 11 Nov 2025:-

1. 1. National Education Day was observed across India on November 11 to commemorate the birth anniversary of Maulana Abul Kalam Azad, the country's first Education Minister.
2. 2. The Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA) handed over the probe into the car explosion near Delhi's Red Fort to the National Investigation Agency (NIA), treating it as an act of terrorism.
3. 3. The G20 Summit 2026 venue was announced as Miami, Florida, USA. The US will take over the G20 Presidency from South Africa on December 1, 2025.
4. 4. The Internal Security Division (ISD) Counter-IED Team of the Karnataka State Police won the 9th National Joint Counter-IED Competition, 'Visfot Kavach IX', for the third consecutive time.
5. 5. India and Saudi Arabia signed a bilateral Hajj agreement for 2026, fixing India's pilgrim quota and committing to upgrade services for the pilgrims.
6. 6. The T20 World Cup 2026 will see its opening match and a semifinal hosted at Mumbai's Wankhede Stadium, while the final will be played at Ahmedabad's Narendra Modi Stadium.
7. 7. China launched a new "K-Visa" category aimed at attracting highly skilled foreign professionals to boost innovation and industry competitiveness.
8. 8. IIM Mumbai launched a new two-year blended MBA programme designed for working professionals, combining online and on-campus learning.
9. 9. Typhoon Fungwong caused devastation in parts of the Philippines, leaving at least 10 people dead and displacing about 1.4 million.
10. 10. The Commission for Air Quality Management (CAQM) imposed the third phase of the Graded Response Action Plan (GRAP) in Delhi-NCR as air quality worsened to the 'severe' category.
11. 11. US flight disruptions worsened amid an ongoing government shutdown, affecting air traffic operations and leading to thousands of flight delays and cancellations.
12. 12. Armistice Day / Veterans Day was observed globally on November 11 in memory of the World War I armistice.
13. 13. The NHAI partnered with Google India to provide near real-time highway information through Google Maps via the API Setu platform, improving highway safety and awareness.
14. 14. The Tamil Nadu government announced the launch of India's inaugural "Women's Wellness on Wheels" (WWW) initiative, aiming to enhance women's health through mobile medical units.
15. 15. AU Small Finance Bank (SFB) launched "M Circle," a women-exclusive banking proposition focused on empowering women through savings, investment, health, and lifestyle benefits.
