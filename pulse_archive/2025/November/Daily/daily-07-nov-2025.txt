Daily Current Affairs [7nov2025]:-

1. 1. 150 Years of Vande Mataram Celebration: Prime Minister Narendra Modi inaugurated the year-long commemoration of India's National Song, Vande Mataram, marking its 150th anniversary (composed by Bankim Chandra Chatterjee in 1875).
2. 2. National Cancer Awareness Day: India observes National Cancer Awareness Day every year on November 7, commemorating the birth anniversary of Nobel laureate Marie Curie.
3. 3. Digital Museum of Tribal Freedom Fighters: PM Modi inaugurated India's first Digital Museum of Tribal Freedom Fighters—the Shaheed Veer Narayan Singh Memorial—in Nava Raipur, Atal Nagar, Chhattisgarh.
4. 4. Indian Hockey Centenary: Centenary celebrations of Indian hockey were held across the country on November 7, 2025, honoring its 100 years of greatness.
5. 5. NHPC Golden Jubilee Coin: A commemorative coin of ₹50 was released to mark the Golden Jubilee of NHPC Limited (a public sector hydropower company).
6. 6. INS Ikshak Commissioned: The indigenously built survey vessel 'Ikshak' was commissioned into the Indian Navy.
7. 7. Urban Mobility India Conference: The 18th Urban Mobility India Conference was inaugurated in Gurugram, where the Dumpsite Remediation Accelerator Programme (DRAP) was launched.
8. 8. MSDE-Autodesk MoU: The Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE) signed an MoU with Autodesk Inc. to enhance digital design and AI skills in India's vocational training ecosystem.
9. 9. RuPay's Growth: RuPay, India's domestic card network, saw its UPI-linked credit card transactions account for 40% of the total transaction volume.
10. 10. King Hamad Lecture: Union Minister for Law and Justice, Arjun Ram Meghwal, represented India at the King Hamad Lecture for Neutral Justice and the Future of International Commercial Courts Conference in Bahrain.
11. 11. Manohar Parrikar Yuva Scientist Award: Dr. Sai Gautam Gopalakrishnan of IISc Bengaluru was conferred the Manohar Parrikar Yuva Scientist Award 2025 for computational materials science.
12. 12. Supreme Court on Stray Dogs: The Supreme Court directed the removal of stray dogs "forthwith" from premises of educational institutions, hospitals, and other public institutions, to be taken to a designated shelter after sterilization and vaccination.
13. 13. India-Israel Defence Cooperation: The 17th India–Israel Joint Working Group (JWG) Meeting on Defence Cooperation was held in Tel Aviv, Israel, focusing on AI, Cyber Security, and R&D.
14. 14. Geothermal Energy Project: Energy Efficiency Services Limited (EESL) announced plans to establish India's largest Geothermal Energy Technology (GET) pilot project in Andhra Pradesh.
15. 15. DoSJE-PW Foundation MoU: The Department of Social Justice & Empowerment (DoSJE) signed an MoU with the Physics Wallah Foundation (PW Foundation) to provide free online coaching for competitive exams to aspirants from Backward Classes.
