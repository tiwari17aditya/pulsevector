Daily Current Affairs November 5, 2025

1. 1. World Tsunami Awareness Day 2025:- Observed globally on November 5, with the theme "Be Tsunami Ready: Invest in Tsunami Preparedness," promoting early warning systems and community readiness, an initiative originally taken by Japan.
2. 2. RDI Scheme Fund Launch:- Prime Minister Narendra Modi launched the ₹1 lakh crore Research, Development and Innovation (RDI) Scheme Fund at the Emerging Science and Technology Innovation Conclave (ESTIC) 2025 in New Delhi, aimed at promoting a private sector-driven R&D ecosystem.
3. 3. End of Import Freeze:- India is set to resume approvals for importing goods from China and other countries, ending a five-year import freeze imposed in 2020, marking a significant policy shift to fast-track pending import proposals.
4. 4. Kerala: First Extreme-Poverty-Free State:- Kerala was officially declared as India's first extreme-poverty-free state on November 1, 2025 (Kerala Formation Day), a result of the state's Extreme Poverty Eradication Project (EPEP) launched in 2021.
5. 5. New Atal Tinkering Labs & PMRFs:- The government announced the establishment of 25,000 new Atal Tinkering Labs (ATLs) and 10,000 additional Prime Minister's Research Fellowships (PMRFs) over the next five years to strengthen India's research talent in emerging fields like AI and Quantum Computing.
6. 6. NITI Aayog's Agri-Tech Roadmap:- NITI Aayog's Frontier Tech Hub unveiled a roadmap titled 'Reimagining Agriculture: A Roadmap for Frontier Technology Led Transformation' in Gandhinagar, Gujarat, in collaboration with BCG, Google, and CII, to boost Agri-tech innovation using AI, digital twins, and climate-resilient seeds.
7. 7. PM Inaugurates NEST Cluster at IIT Guwahati:- PM Modi inaugurated the NEST Cluster (North East Science & Technology Cluster) at IIT Guwahati with an investment of ₹22.98 crore, focusing on four verticals: Grassroots Innovation, Semiconductors & AI, Bamboo-Based Technologies, and Biodegradable Plastics.
8. 8. IPPB-EPFO MoU:- India Post Payments Bank (IPPB) and Employees' Provident Fund Organisation (EPFO) signed an MoU to provide doorstep issuance of Jeevan Pramaan Certificate (JPC) for EPS'95 pensioners free of cost.
9. 9. CGDA and Chief of Materiel Appointments:- Vishvajit Sahay assumed charge as the Controller General of Defence Accounts (CGDA), and Vice Admiral B. Sivakumar took charge as the 40th Chief of Materiel of the Indian Navy, marking key leadership reshuffles in defence.
10. 10. Digital Marine Fisheries Census:- Union Minister George Kurian launched the Fully Digital Marine Fisheries Census 2025 with the VyAS Apps, which will cover 1.2 million fisher households across coastal states over a 45-day period.
11. 11. New York City Mayor:- Zohran Mamdani was elected as New York City's new Mayor on November 4, 2025, becoming the city's first Muslim mayor and the first person of South Asian descent to hold the office.
12. 12. Hinduja Group Chairman Passes Away:- Gopichand Hinduja, the Chairman of the Hinduja Group, passed away in London at the age of 85.
13. 13. Bank of Baroda's Milestones:- Bank of Baroda (BoB) completed its first-ever gold trade as a Special Category Client (SCC) on the India International Bullion Exchange (IIBX).
14. 14. Defence Deal: HAL and GE:- Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL) and GE have been negotiating the procurement of 113 F404 engines to power the LCA Mk 1As and finalising the joint production of F414 engines for the LCA Mk2 jets.
15. 15. India's Rank in WJP Rule of Law Index 2025:- India was ranked 86th in the World Justice Project (WJP) Rule of Law Index 2025, with concerns highlighted over judicial delays, government accountability, and civil liberties.
