Daily Current Affairs 30 Nov 2025:-

1. 1. Mann Ki Baat 128th Edition: PM Modi addressed the nation, lauding India's ICC Women's World Cup win and the nation's progress in the space sector, citing the induction of INS Mahé and the unveiling of Skyroot's Infinity Campus.
2. 2. Smart Cities Mission: Tamil Nadu leads the state-wise ranking for the Smart Cities Mission with 12 smart cities, followed by Uttar Pradesh and Maharashtra (10 each).
3. 3. Defence Milestone: The Prime Minister mentioned the recent induction of INS Mahé into the Indian Navy, a new Anti-Submarine Warfare Shallow Water Craft (ASW-SWC).
4. 4. Agricultural Record: India achieved a historic record with a massive foodgrain production of 357 million tonnes, an increase of 100 million tonnes compared to a decade ago.
5. 5. New Private Rocket: PM Modi unveiled Vikram-1, which is India's first private orbital rocket.
6. 6. UN Water Convention: Bangladesh became the first South Asian nation to accede to the UN Water Convention in 2025, which has implications for transboundary water cooperation.
7. 7. Tex-RAMPS Scheme: The Government of India approved the Tex-RAMPS scheme to promote research and innovation within the Indian textile sector.
8. 8. International Day: World AIDS Day is set to be observed on December 1st with the 2025 theme: "Overcoming disruption, transforming the AIDS response."
9. 9. Women in Sports: PM Modi highlighted the Indian women's team's victories in the ICC Women's World Cup, Kabaddi World Cup, and the Blind Cricket World Cup.
10. 10. Global First in Aviation: The world's largest leap engine MRO (Maintenance, Repair, and Overhaul) facility was inaugurated in Hyderabad, reflecting a new thinking in the aviation sector.
11. 11. Nepal's New Currency: Nepal issued new ₹100 currency notes featuring disputed regions claimed by India (Kalapani, Lipulekh, Limpiyadhura).
12. 12. Cabinet Approval: The Union Cabinet approved a ₹7,280 crore scheme to promote the production of Sintered Rare Earth Permanent Magnets.
13. 13. PNB Initiative: Punjab National Bank (PNB) launched a new digital solution under the government's Ek Bharat: Shreshtha Bharat scheme.
14. 14. Cricket Ambassador: The ICC appointed a prominent cricketer as the Ambassador for the T20 World Cup 2026.
15. 15. WHO Report on TB: The WHO Global TB Report 2025 noted that India has achieved a 21% reduction in TB incidence, marking a significant milestone towards a TB-Mukt Bharat.
