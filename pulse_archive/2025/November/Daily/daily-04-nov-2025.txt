Daily Current Affairs November 4, 2025

1. 1. Chhattisgarh Train Collision: A tragic train collision near Bilaspur railway station in Chhattisgarh resulted in the death of at least four people and multiple injuries after a passenger train collided with a goods train. Initial reports suggested the passenger train overshot a red signal.
2. 2. NE Political Entity: Political leaders from the Northeast, including Conrad Sangma, Pradyot Manikya Debbarma, and Kikon, announced the formation of a new common political entity for the region.
3. 3. Online Gaming Law: The Supreme Court orally indicated that regular competitions and tournaments might be excluded from the ambit of the Promotion and Regulation of Online Gaming Act, 2025, as they may not fall under the definition of 'betting and gambling'.
4. 4. EPFO's Employee Enrollment Scheme 2025: The Employees' Provident Fund Organization (EPFO) celebrated its 73rd Foundation Day by launching the Employee Enrollment Scheme 2025, which aims to encourage employers to voluntarily declare and enrol eligible employees under the Employees' Provident Fund (EPF).
5. 5. Election Commission's Special Intensive Revision (SIR): The Election Commission of India initiated the Special Intensive Revision (SIR) of electoral rolls starting November 4, 2025, in 12 states and union territories to update voter lists, a process which faced a challenge from DMK in the Supreme Court.
6. 6. India-US/India-Bahrain Defense Ties: India and the US signed a new 10-year defense cooperation agreement to enhance strategic ties and technology sharing, while India and Bahrain agreed to boost cooperation in fighting terrorism and security.
7. 7. French Honour for Sunil Kant Munjal: Sunil Kant Munjal was conferred with France's prestigious Chevalier de l'Ordre National du Mérite for his contributions to industry and philanthropy.
8. 8. ISRO Agreement on Coal Fires: ISRO signed a tripartite agreement with BCCL (Bharat Coking Coal Limited) and CMPDI (Central Mine Planning and Design Institute) to conduct a study on coal fires in the Jharia Coalfield in Jharkhand.
9. 9. RBI November 2025 Key Surveys: The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) launched the November 2025 rounds of three key surveys: Inflation Expectations Survey of Households (IESH), Consumer Confidence Survey (RCCS - Rural and Semi-urban areas), and Urban Consumer Confidence Survey (UCCS), providing critical inputs for Monetary Policy.
10. 10. Kane Williamson T20I Retirement: New Zealand cricket star Kane Williamson announced his retirement from the T20I Cricket format.
11. 11. Pink Saheli Smart Card: The Delhi government launched the 'Pink Saheli Smart Card' for free bus travel for women and transgender individuals aged 12 years and above on DTC and cluster buses.
12. 12. Cyclone Alert for Andaman & Nicobar: The India Meteorological Department (IMD) issued a Cyclone Alert for the Andaman and Nicobar Islands due to a cyclonic circulation over the Bay of Bengal, which was likely to intensify from November 4.
13. 13. UNESCO November 4 Events: UNESCO held events including the launch of the UNESCO in Action for Gender Equality 2024–2025 report and the presentation of the World Trends Report on Freedom of Expression and Media Development.
14. 14. US SNAP Benefits: US politician Donald Trump stated that SNAP (Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program) benefits would resume only after the US government reopens, amidst a funding deadlock in Washington.
15. 15. Iran Hostage Crisis Anniversary: November 4, 2025, marked the 46th anniversary of the Iran hostage crisis when 66 Americans were taken hostage at the US Embassy in Tehran in 1979.
