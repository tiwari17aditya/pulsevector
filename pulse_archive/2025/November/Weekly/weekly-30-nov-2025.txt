Weekly Current Affairs ( 24 Nov - 30 Nov 2025 )

1. 1. 53rd CJI Appointment: Justice Surya Kant was sworn in as the 53rd Chief Justice of India on November 24, 2025.
2. 2. Constitution Day: India celebrated Constitution Day (Samvidhan Diwas) on November 26, commemorating the adoption of the Constitution in 1949.
3. 3. G20 Summit 2025: The G20 Leaders' Summit 2025 concluded in South Africa, marking the first time the summit was hosted on the African continent.
4. 4. Defence Milestone: The new Anti-Submarine Warfare Shallow Water Craft (ASW-SWC), INS Mahé, was officially inducted into the Indian Navy.
5. 5. Aviation MRO: PM Modi virtually inaugurated the Safran Aircraft Engine Services India (SAESI) facility in Hyderabad, India’s first global-level MRO.
6. 6. Asia Power Index: India secured the third rank in the Asia Power Index 2025, released by the Lowy Institute, behind the US and China.
7. 7. New Cyclone: A cyclonic storm named 'Ditwah' formed over the Bay of Bengal; the name was suggested by Yemen.
8. 8. Space Ecosystem Boost: India's private space sector was highlighted with the inauguration of Skyroot's Infinity Campus and the mention of the Vikram-1 private orbital rocket.
9. 9. Police Conference: The 60th All India Conference of Directors General/Inspectors General of Police was held in Raipur, Chhattisgarh.
10. 10. Global Film Festival: The 56th International Film Festival of India (IFFI) 2025 concluded its proceedings in Goa.
11. 11. Textile Scheme: The Government approved the Tex-RAMPS scheme to promote research and innovation in the Indian textile industry.
12. 12. New University in Punjab: Punjab CM Bhagwant Mann announced a world-class university named after Sri Guru Tegh Bahadur Ji at Sri Anandpur Sahib.
13. 13. India's First Antibiotic: Dr. Jitendra Singh announced the soft launch of India's first indigenous antibiotic, Nafithromycin, to combat drug resistance.
14. 14. GI Tag: Ambaji Marble from Gujarat, known for its milky white color, received the Geographical Indication (GI) tag.
15. 15. WHO Report on TB: The WHO Global TB Report 2025 acknowledged India's significant 21% reduction in TB incidence.
16. 16. IMF Grade: The International Monetary Fund (IMF) retained its 'C' grade for India's national account statistics, noting scope for improvement.
17. 17. Constitutional Bill: The Constitution (131st Amendment) Bill, 2025, was in the news, proposing to bring Chandigarh under the ambit of Article 240.
18. 18. Freight Loading Record: Indian Railways crossed 1 billion tonnes of freight loading in FY 2025–26, driven by reforms and efficiency.
19. 19. Telecom Regulation: TRAI mandated timelines for banks and financial entities to adopt the '1600' numbering series for their services.
20. 20. Cabinet Approval: The Union Cabinet approved a ₹7,280 crore scheme to promote Sintered Rare Earth Permanent Magnets production.
21. 21. Environment Day: International Day for the Elimination of Violence against Women was observed on November 25th.
22. 22. PNB Initiative: Punjab National Bank launched a new digital solution under the government's Ek Bharat: Shreshtha Bharat scheme.
23. 23. Sardar@150 March: The national foot march named 'Sardar@150 Unity March' was flagged off in Gujarat.
24. 24. Bank News: Axis Bank put its exposure to Lavasa Corp up for sale to recover dues.
25. 25. Radio Expansion: Radio broadcasting started from a newly established Akashvani centre in the holy city of Ujjain, Madhya Pradesh.
26. 26. Global Summit: The COP30 Summit on climate change concluded in Belem, Brazil.
27. 27. New Currency: Nepal issued new ₹100 currency notes featuring disputed regions claimed by India.
28. 28. Blue Flag Beaches: Sunapur Beach and Puri Golden Beach in Odisha secured the Blue Flag Certification for 2025–26.
29. 29. SCO Meeting: The Shanghai Cooperation Organisation (SCO) Foreign Ministers' meeting was a major diplomatic event during the week.
30. 30. Deepest Lab Plan: India announced plans to build the world's deepest underwater research laboratory at 6,000 metres in the Indian Ocean.
