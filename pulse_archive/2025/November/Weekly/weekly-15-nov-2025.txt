Weekly Current Affairs ( 10 Nov - 15 Nov 2025 )

1. 1. Bihar Assembly Elections 2025: The BJP-JDU alliance secured a commanding win in the Bihar Assembly elections, with Nitish Kumar likely to be sworn in for his 10th term as the Chief Minister.
2. 2. National Counter-IED Championship: The Internal Security Division (ISD) Counter-IED Team of the Karnataka State Police won the 9th National Joint Counter-IED Competition, 'Visfot Kavach IX', for the third consecutive time.
3. 3. SEBI Chief on Investors: SEBI Chairperson Tuhin Kanta Pandey noted the growing confidence of Indian investors, with India now having 13.6 crore investors and 21 crore demat accounts.
4. 4. AMRIT Pharmacy Initiative: The Affordable Medicines and Reliable Implants for Treatment (AMRIT) initiative, completed 10 years, with Health Minister J.P. Nadda announcing its nationwide expansion.
5. 5. Strait of Hormuz: The Strait of Hormuz, which connects the Persian Gulf with the Arabian Sea via the Gulf of Oman, was in the news due to regional tensions.
6. 6. Palar River Origin: The Palar River, a river in southern India with major tributaries like the Cheyyar River and Ponnai River, originates in Karnataka and drains into the Bay of Bengal.
7. 7. Man-Portable Autonomous Underwater Vehicle (MP-AUV): Developed by the Naval Science & Technological Laboratory (NSTL), Visakhapatnam (DRDO), primarily for mine countermeasure missions.
8. 8. Ambaji Marble: This type of marble, known for its milky white colour, is predominantly quarried in Ambaji, Gujarat.
9. 9. Sickle Cell Disease Gene Therapy: The UK became the first country to approve gene therapy treatment for Sickle Cell Disease (SCD).
10. 10. Pakistan's 27th Constitutional Amendment: The President of Pakistan signed the 27th Constitutional Amendment Act, which reportedly grants supreme authority to the Military Chief.
11. 11. JPC Chairperson Appointment: Aparajita Sarangi has been appointed as the Chairperson of the Joint Parliamentary Committee (JPC), which consists of 31 members.
12. 12. G20 Summit 2026 Venue: It was announced that the G20 Summit in 2026 will be held in Miami, Florida, USA.
13. 13. India's Gold Repatriation: The RBI repatriated 274 tonnes of India's gold between March 2023 and September 2025 to strengthen reserves and reduce custodial risks, raising domestic holdings to 575.8 tonnes.
14. 14. Quad Naval Drills: INS Sahyadri participated in key Quad Naval Drills in Guam to boost interoperability and maritime security in the Indo-Pacific.
15. 15. China's K-Visa: China introduced a new "K-Visa" category aimed at attracting highly skilled foreign professionals.
16. 16. T20 World Cup 2026 Venues: The ICC announced that the opening match and a semifinal of the T20 World Cup 2026 will be hosted at Mumbai's Wankhede Stadium, while the final will be played at Ahmedabad's Narendra Modi Stadium.
17. 17. Dumpsite Remediation Accelerator Programme (DRAP): Launched under the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs (MoHUA) for fast-tracked scientific capping, biomining, and bio-remediation of waste to meet the 2026 deadline.
18. 18. Cooperative Kumbh 2025: An international conference on the urban cooperative credit sector, held in New Delhi, organized by the Ministry of Cooperation.
19. 19. India's TB Elimination Target: The Global TB Report 2025 showed that India has achieved a 21% reduction in new cases (2015-2024), but is still far from its ambitious elimination target of 2025 (less than one case per million population).
20. 20. Shah Rukh Khan's Property: Shah Rukh Khan unveiled a 56-storey tower named 'Shahrukhz' in Dubai, the first property ever named after him.
21. 21. World Science Day for Peace and Development: Observed on November 10, with the theme "Unleash Your Inner Scientist".
22. 22. Uttarakhand Anniversary: Uttarakhand marked its 25th Anniversary with PM Modi announcing development projects worth over ₹8,000 Cr.
23. 23. India-Korea Shipbuilding Model: Union Minister Hardeep Singh Puri suggested the India-Korea cooperation model for vessel building can serve world markets.
24. 24. Tropical Forests Forever Facility (TFFF): A Brazil-led global fund to reward tropical countries for protecting forests, which India has joined as an observer.
25. 25. Ramman Festival: An 18-mask Ramayana performance festival from Uttarakhand, recognised by UNESCO as an Intangible Cultural Heritage.
26. 26. Net-Zero Emissions Target: China aims to become net-zero by 2060, while India plans to achieve it by 2070.
27. 27. GE F404 Engines Deal: HAL signed an agreement with GE for the supply of 113 F404 engines to power the LCA Mk 1A programme.
28. 28. India-Bhutan Railway Projects: PM Modi is expected to review progress on the Kokrajhar–Gelephu and Banarhat–Samtse railway links.
29. 29. Vulture Population in India: A report found that vultures have disappeared from 71.7% of 425 historical nesting sites, with a total breeding population of ~4,800 vultures across India.
30. 30. Hepatitis A Transmission: The disease is primarily transmitted by the faecal-oral route, and there is currently no specific antiviral treatment.
