Weekly Current Affairs ( November 03 - November 09, 2025 )

1. 1. PM Modi in Uttarakhand: Prime Minister Narendra Modi inaugurated and laid the foundation stone for various projects worth over ₹8,260 crore in Uttarakhand to mark the 25th anniversary of the state's formation on November 9.
2. 2. National Urban Conclave 2025: The two-day conclave on "Sustainable Urban Development and Governance" concluded in New Delhi, where the Union Minister launched the Dumpsite Remediation Accelerator Programme (DRAP) and the Swachh Bharat Mission – Knowledge Management Unit (KMU).
3. 3. National Legal Services Day: Observed annually on November 9 to commemorate the enactment of the Legal Services Authorities Act, 1987, and promote legal awareness and free legal aid.
4. 4. Gujarat ATS Arrests: The Gujarat Anti-Terrorism Squad (ATS) arrested three suspects for allegedly conspiring to carry out terrorist attacks across the country, seizing weapons from them.
5. 5. HAL and GE Agreement: Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL) signed a fresh agreement worth approximately USD 1 billion with US-based General Electric (GE) for the supply of 113 F404 engines to power 97 Tejas Mk1A jets.
6. 6. UIDAI's Aadhaar Vision 2032: The Unique Identification Authority of India (UIDAI) launched 'Aadhaar Vision 2032', a long-term roadmap to modernize India's Aadhaar ecosystem with advanced digital technologies.
7. 7. Lucknow UNESCO Creative City: Lucknow has been designated as a UNESCO Creative City of Gastronomy at the 43rd UNESCO General Conference in Samarkand, Uzbekistan, recognizing its Awadhi culinary heritage.
8. 8. Gogabeel Lake Ramsar Site: Gogabeel Lake, located in the Katihar district of Bihar, has been declared a Ramsar Site, becoming India's 94th wetland of international importance.
9. 9. Janjatiya Gaurav Varsh Pakhwada: The Eklavya Model Residential Schools (EMRSs) are observing the Janjatiya Gaurav Varsh Pakhwada (Tribal Pride Fortnight) from November 1st to 15th, 2025.
10. 10. Digital Arrest Scams: Reports highlight the rising menace of digital arrest scams, with a 72% increase in impersonation cases in 2024 compared to 2023, often targeting senior citizens.
11. 11. BIMSTEC-India Marine Research Network (BIMReN): The first conference of BIMReN was held in Kochi, Kerala, with the objective to strengthen scientific cooperation in marine research among BIMSTEC nations.
12. 12. ISRO's PSLV Transfer: ISRO is planning to partially transfer the development and production of the Polar Satellite Launch Vehicle (PSLV) to an Indian industry consortium led by HAL and L&T.
13. 13. India's 91st Chess Grandmaster: Raahul V S became India's 91st Grandmaster (GM) at the 6th ASEAN Individual Chess Championship in the Philippines.
14. 14. TEC and IIT Bombay MoU: The Telecommunication Engineering Centre (TEC) and IIT Bombay signed an MoU to develop India-specific telecom standards, test frameworks, and explore next-generation telecom technologies like 6G and AI.
15. 15. China Eastern's Shanghai-Delhi Flight: China Eastern Airlines resumed its Shanghai-Delhi service with high occupancy, becoming the first mainland Chinese carrier this year to resume direct flights to India after five years.
16. 16. India's Electronics Export Boom: India's electronic sector has emerged as a new growth frontier, with exports crossing $40 billion (FY25) and forming over 10% of India's total exports.
17. 17. Adi-L1 CME Observations: Scientists at the Indian Institute of Astrophysics (IIA) and NASA collaborated to record the very first spectroscopic observations of a Coronal Mass Ejection (CME) in the visible wavelength range using the VELC payload on India's Aditya-L1 mission.
18. 18. Tropical Forests Forever Facility (TFFF): India has joined as an observer to the TFFF, a Brazil-led global fund aimed at mobilizing USD 125 billion to reward tropical countries for protecting and expanding forests.
19. 19. CIL and DVC JV: Coal India Limited (CIL) and Damodar Valley Corporation (DVC) signed a strategic Joint Venture agreement for the brownfield expansion of the thermal power project in Chandrapura, TPS in Jharkhand.
20. 20. ReNew Financial Package: Renewable energy company ReNew secured a financial package of USD 477 million from the Asian Development Bank (ADB) to advance India's renewable energy infrastructure.
21. 21. New START Treaty: Russian Foreign Minister Sergei Lavrov stated that the US is reviewing President Putin's proposal to maintain the limitations outlined in the New Strategic Arms Reduction Treaty (New START) beyond its scheduled expiration in February 2026.
22. 22. LGM-30G Minuteman III ICBM Test: The US Air Force Global Strike Command (AFGSC) and Space Force conducted a test launch of the LGM-30G Minuteman III Intercontinental Ballistic Missile (ICBM).
23. 23. Glide Phase Interceptor (GPI): The US and Japan are jointly developing the GPI missile-intercepting system, aiming to achieve full operational capability by the end of 2032.
24. 24. Fujian Aircraft Carrier: Fujian is highlighted as China's first aircraft carrier with a catapult launch system, enabling it to launch heavy and fully loaded warplanes.
25. 25. WSSD-2 Doha Political Declaration: The 2nd World Summit for Social Development (WSSD-2) concluded in Doha, Qatar, with the adoption of the Doha Political Declaration, pledging to eradicate poverty and expand social security.
26. 26. Bharat Ratna Dr. Bhupen Hazarika National Awards 2025: Union Minister Jyotiraditya Scindia presented the awards in Guwahati, Assam, to six eminent personalities from Northeast India.
27. 27. Kerala Extreme Poverty Free: Kerala has become India's first state to declare itself free from extreme poverty under the Extreme Poverty Eradication Project (EPEP) initiative.
28. 28. Marine Fisheries Census 2025: Union Minister Shri George Kurian launched the fully digital Marine Fisheries Census 2025 along with the VyAS Apps.
29. 29. Pink Saheli Smart Card: The Delhi Government launched the 'Pink Saheli Smart Card' for free bus travel for women and transgender individuals.
30. 30. Nauradehi Sanctuary Cheetahs: Nauradehi Sanctuary in Madhya Pradesh is set to become the third home for Cheetahs in India.
