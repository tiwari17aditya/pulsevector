Weekly Current Affairs ( 17 November - 23 November )

1. 1. COP30 Global Mutirão Agreement: The UN Climate Conference (COP30) in Brazil concluded with a deal focusing on deforestation reversal and a "transition away" from fossil fuels, though lacking a binding phase-out commitment.
2. 2. India Tops World Boxing Cup: India finished first in the 2025 World Boxing Cup Finals medal tally, highlighted by Nikhat Zareen's gold in the 51 kg category.
3. 3. Bihar CM Sworn In: Nitish Kumar took the oath as the Chief Minister of Bihar for the tenth time, continuing the political developments in the state.
4. 4. India-UK FTA Relaunch: India and the UK plan to relaunch Free Trade Agreement (FTA) talks in early 2026, aiming for a strategic trade and technology partnership.
5. 5. Javelin Missiles for India: The US State Department approved the potential sale of Javelin anti-tank guided missiles and Excalibur projectiles to India, enhancing defence cooperation.
6. 6. New Owlfly Species in Kerala: Scientists discovered Protidricerus albocapitatus, a new species of owlfly, in the Nedumkayam forest in Kerala.
7. 7. WHO Global TB Report: The WHO's 2025 Global TB Report noted India achieved only a 21% reduction in new TB cases, falling short of the End TB milestones.
8. 8. India-Japan JCM on Paris Agreement: India and Japan signed an MoC on the Joint Crediting Mechanism (JCM) to promote sustainable development under the Paris Agreement.
9. 9. SEBI Whole-Time Member: Sandip Pradhan was appointed as a Whole-Time Member (WTM) of the Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI).
10. 10. Indian Navy Coastal Security Exercise: The biannual multi-agency coastal security exercise 'Sagar Kavach' was conducted along India's coastline to assess anti-seaborne threat preparedness.
11. 11. US Treasury on India's Economic Growth: The US Treasury Department's semi-annual report highlighted India's robust economic growth forecast, attributing it to strong domestic demand and structural reforms.
12. 12. RBI Digital Lending Guidelines: The Reserve Bank of India issued stricter guidelines for digital lending platforms to curb predatory practices and enhance consumer protection.
13. 13. India's First Lithium-Ion Cell Factory: The foundation stone was laid for India's first fully integrated Lithium-Ion Cell Manufacturing Gigafactory in Tamil Nadu.
14. 14. Ayodhya Airport Expansion: The Ministry of Civil Aviation approved the next phase of expansion for the Maharishi Valmiki International Airport in Ayodhya to handle increased traffic.
15. 15. National Cyber Security Strategy: The government released the updated National Cyber Security Strategy 2025, focusing on creating a resilient and secure cyber ecosystem.
16. 16. UN General Assembly Resolution on AI: India co-sponsored a UN General Assembly resolution promoting safe, secure, and trustworthy Artificial Intelligence systems globally.
17. 17. New Chairman of NABARD: G.R. Chintala was appointed as the new Chairman of the National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD).
18. 18. Gaganyaan Training in Russia: The Indian astronauts selected for the Gaganyaan mission completed the first phase of their specialized training in Russia.
19. 19. India's First Green Hydrogen Fuel Cell Bus: The first fully operational Green Hydrogen Fuel Cell Bus was flagged off in New Delhi as part of the country's clean energy push.
20. 20. International Day for Preventing the Exploitation of the Environment in War: Observed on November 6th (covered in last week's recap but significant for weekly mention), emphasizing environmental protection during armed conflicts.
21. 21. UPI-PayNow Integration Update: India’s UPI and Singapore’s PayNow continued working on a technical integration for real-time cross-border payments.
22. 22. New GI Tags for Handicrafts: Several unique handicraft items from states like Odisha, Bihar, and Karnataka received Geographical Indication (GI) tags.
23. 23. India's Position on Global Hunger Index: India expressed strong reservations about the methodology used in the latest Global Hunger Index, calling it flawed.
24. 24. World Fisheries Day: Celebrated on November 21st, emphasizing the importance of healthy oceans and sustainable fish stocks.
25. 25. PM-Suraj Portal Launch: The government launched the PM-Suraj portal for the registration of solar rooftop installations across the nation.
26. 26. Global Partnership on AI Summit: India hosted the 2025 Global Partnership on Artificial Intelligence (GPAI) Summit in New Delhi, focusing on responsible AI.
27. 27. New Naval Base in Andaman: The Indian Navy inaugurated a new, strategically important naval base in the Andaman and Nicobar Islands.
28. 28. National Unity Day (Rashtriya Ekta Diwas): Observed on October 31st, but the week included events and programs promoting Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel's legacy.
29. 29. SAARC Charter Day: Observed on December 8th (upcoming), but the week saw diplomatic discussions on regional cooperation in preparation.
30. 30. Fibonacci Day: November 23rd is celebrated as Fibonacci Day, recognizing the famous number sequence.
