Daily Current Affairs 6 January 2025:-

1. World Day of War Orphans 2025 observed globally to raise awareness for children in conflict zones.
2. Guru Gobind Singh Jayanti celebrated across India marking the 358th birth anniversary of the Sikh Guru.
3. Eight security personnel killed in an IED blast by Naxals in Bijapur, Chhattisgarh.
4. PM Modi inaugurates the new Jammu Railway Division to enhance regional connectivity.
5. RBI approves merger of National Co-operative Bank with Cosmos Co-operative Bank.
6. India’s first ‘Generation Beta’ baby born in Aizawl, Mizoram, marking a new demographic cohort.
7. French Carrier Charles de Gaulle docks in Goa for the bilateral Naval Exercise Varuna.
8. 82nd Golden Globe Awards held in Los Angeles honoring excellence in film and television.
9. HMPV virus cases detected in Karnataka and Gujarat; Health Ministry issues advisory.
10. Union Steel Minister launches PLI Scheme 1.1 to boost domestic specialty steel production.
11. Odisha CM inaugurates the Adivasi Mela 2025 in Bhubaneswar showcasing tribal culture and products.
12. Sensex plunges over 1,000 points due to weak global cues and HMPV virus fears.
13. ISRO postpones the SpaDeX docking experiment to January 9 for final technical validation.
14. Nuclear scientist Dr. R. Chidambaram passes away at 88; instrumental in India's Pokhran tests.
15. Winter Char Dham circuit inaugurated in Uttarakhand to promote year-round pilgrimage tourism.
