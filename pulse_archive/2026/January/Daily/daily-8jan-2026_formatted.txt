Daily Current Affairs 08 January 2026

1. GDP Projection: India's real GDP growth for 2025-26 is projected at 7.4% by MoSPI's first advance estimate.
2. Economic Milestone: India's nominal GDP reached ₹357 lakh crore, bringing the nation close to the $4-trillion economy mark.
3. Railway Electrification: Indian Railways became the world's largest electrified rail system, achieving 99% electrification of broad-gauge networks.
4. PPP Pipeline: The Centre created a three-year Public Private Partnership pipeline worth ₹17 lakh crore for infrastructure projects.
5. ISRO Mission: ISRO confirmed via Dust Experiment (DEX) that interplanetary dust enters Earth's atmosphere every 1,000 seconds.
6. AI in Healthcare: India’s first government hospital-based AI clinic inaugurated at GIMS, Greater Noida for advanced genetic screening.
7. IIT Madras Global: External Affairs Minister Dr. S. Jaishankar launched the IITM Global Research Foundation to expand international presence.
8. Billiards Loss: Two-time World Billiards Champion and national coach Manoj Kothari passed away at the age of 67.
9. Infrastructure Project: West Bengal CM Mamata Banerjee laid the foundation for the 5-km Sagar Island Bridge over Muriganga river.
10. Hydropower News: Union Environment Ministry panel recommended clearance for the 1,200 MW Kalai-II hydroelectric project in Arunachal Pradesh.
11. Sports Retirement: Asian Games gold medalist and national record-holder runner Jinson Johnson announced his retirement from professional athletics.
12. Cricket Update: India Under-19 team defeated South Africa by 233 runs to win the Youth ODI series 3-0.
13. Literature Launch: Goa CM Pramod Sawant released the second edition of "History That India Ignored" authored by Prem Prakash.
14. Badminton Success: PV Sindhu advanced to the second round of the Malaysia Open after defeating Chinese Taipei's Sung Shuo Yun.
15. Global Observance: January 8th is observed globally as Earth’s Rotation Day, commemorating Leon Foucault’s 1851 demonstration.
