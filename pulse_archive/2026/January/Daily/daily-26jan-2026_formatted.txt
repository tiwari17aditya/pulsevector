title :- Daily Current Affairs 26 January 2026 :-

1. India celebrates 77th Republic Day on January 26, 2026, commemorating the Constitution's adoption in 1950.
2. The central theme for the 2026 parade is "150 Years of Vande Mataram" honoring the national song.
3. European Commission President Ursula von der Leyen and European Council President António Costa attended as Chief Guests.
4. President Droupadi Murmu unfurled the Tricolour and took the salute at Kartavya Path, New Delhi.
5. Prime Minister Narendra Modi paid homage to fallen heroes at the National War Memorial before the parade.
6. The Indian Army showcased a first-ever "phased Battle Array" display featuring drones, tanks, and realistic combat formations.
7. President Murmu conferred the Ashoka Chakra, India's highest peacetime gallantry award, on Sudhanshu Shukla.
8. DRDO displayed advanced defense technologies, including the Long-Range Anti-Ship Missile (LR-AShM) and Hypersonic Glide Missile.
9. Around 2,500 artists performed a special cultural tribute titled "Swatantrata ka Mantra: Vande Mataram" at Kartavya Path.
10. A total of 30 tableaux from States and Ministries showcased heritage, development, and India's "Jan Bhagidari" spirit.
11. Chhattisgarh's tableau featured India's first digital museum dedicated to tribal freedom fighters and their sacrifices.
12. Kerala highlighted its Water Metro and 100% digital literacy achievement under the "Aatmanirbhar Kerala" theme.
13. An all-women contingent of CRPF and SSB performed daredevil motorcycle formations, demonstrating women’s empowerment in forces.
14. The Indian Air Force's flypast featured Rafale, Su-30 MKI, and the newly inducted C-295 transport aircraft.
15. The 16th India-EU Summit will be co-chaired by PM Modi and EU leaders in New Delhi tomorrow.
